Main responsibilities

  • Job description Taking on this project-related role They focus on the following tasks and responsibilities:
  • Construction & Operations Management: Responsibility for the operational management of the Living Lab HIL project office and the establishment of efficient, transparent PMO processes.
  • Planning: Overarching schedule, milestone and resource planning for the interdisciplinary overall project in close coordination with the planning team, internal ETH participants and external project partners.
  • Stakeholder Management: Central hub for the flow of information and communication with internal and external stakeholders (professorships, ETH Real Estate, external implementation partners e.g.
  • Gesamtleister, specialist planner).
  • Resource Allocation & Controlling: Strategic allocation of human and financial resources as well as ongoing budget controlling in cooperation with the managing director and accounting.
  • Contract & Document Management: Structuring, auditing and reliable management of contracts, agreements and central project documents.
  • Publications and reporting: coordination and cooperation in the preparation of reports and presentations together with the internal communications office.
  • Session management: organisation, preparation and follow-up of meetings, workshops and committees, including protocol and pendences in German and English.
  • Team leadership: leadership of the PMO team in coordination with management.
