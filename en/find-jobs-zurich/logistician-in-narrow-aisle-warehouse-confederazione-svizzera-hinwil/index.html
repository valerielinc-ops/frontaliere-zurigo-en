Description

  • Responsible for the proper conduct of the work and assigned military personnel and/or assigned civilian personnel in the surrender and return of army vehicles and equipment to the force
  • Professionally store, remove and relocate the material and provide or ship it in accordance with existing regulations and instructions
  • Allocation of the material responsible for repair and inspection and check and ensure its condition and the functionality of the material stored above and below ground
  • Instruct and train the troops and specialists about the treatment and maintenance of the material as part of the material takeover of army systems and army material
  • Inventory management, inventory management and re-ordering
  • Completed vocational apprenticeship as a logistician or equivalent training
  • Good MS Office and SAP user knowledge for use in warehouse management, inventory management and re-orders
  • Driving licence of category B, C and forklift, CE and A advantageous
  • Frequent and conscientious work and organisational skills to support a smooth operation
  • Responsible handling of SAP materials management and pronounced suitability for customer contact and consulting
