PhD opening in material science of stone conservation

100%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken

The Chair for Physical Chemistry of Building Materials has one opening for PhD. position on the topic of stone conservation, in particular the mechanisms of contour scaling.

  • PhD opening in material science of stone conservation
  • 100%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken

Additional details

  • 100%, Zurich, fixed-term
  • The selected student will collaborate with another PhD candidate funded in the scheme and employed at the Chair for Digital Building Technologies in the Department of Architecture. Project background
  • The 3D printing will begin using an innovative and large-scale 3D powder bed printer developed by the Chair for Digital Building Technologies, and will be advanced for the purpose of this project by the other PhD student.
  • That study will be complemented by method development to enable rapid detection of contour scales on site, in addition to exploring possible means of halting the propagation and/or formation of such contour scales
  • As the project involves collaboration between groups in addition to field practitioners, very good communication skills and willingness to collaborate with other researchers is essential
  • We are committed to diversity by allowing flexibility in working hours, home office, and other measures to ensure team members can adequately perform their roles.
  • Working, teaching and research at ETH Zurich We value diversity and sustainability
