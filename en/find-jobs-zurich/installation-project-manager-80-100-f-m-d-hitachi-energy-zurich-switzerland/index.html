Role overview

In this function, you take over the organization, coordination and management of all assembly, commissioning and maintenance work on site at the Oerlikon site.

You work closely with internal and external customers and ensure that projects are implemented efficiently, securely and with high quality.

Initiation of corrective measures Complete compilation, storage and archiving of all relevant documentation (red marks, test protocols, reports) for as-built creation and billing to the customer With basic technical training as well as several years of experience in the assembly and / or commissioning of GIS systems, you will gain sound technical understanding
