Main responsibilities

  • Key responsibilities
  • Own assigned repair jobs end-to-end: order entry, scope confirmation, inspection reporting, repair execution tracking, final documentation, and on-time delivery.
  • Build and issue technical proposals (inspection reports, repair quotes, supplementary scope) using the LASC standard template, in coordination with the assigned Sales Representative.
  • Coordinate daily with Shop Leads and the Service Center Superintendent on job scheduling, resource allocation, parts availability, and execution priorities.
  • Manage customer communication on job status, inspection findings, photos, scope changes, and revised delivery dates; schedule and confirm customer Witness and Hold Points.
  • Maintain accurate job data in SAP — order status, milestones, hours, materials, costs, and delivery dates — and support backlog reviews and forecasting.
  • Compile and harmonize inspection documentation: BOMs, dimensional reports, photo packages, NDE results, and final repair reports for customer delivery.
  • Coordinate with Engineering on technical clarifications, non-standard repairs, and engineered solutions.
  • Support Quality (ISO 9001) and EHS (ISO 14001 / 45001) procedures across the jobs you own; participate in nonconformity tracking and corrective actions when applicable.
  • Coordinate logistics: inbound equipment receipt, parts procurement follow-up, and outbound shipping to the customer. Required qualifications

Key requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent; technical or engineering education a strong plus. Preferred qualifications
