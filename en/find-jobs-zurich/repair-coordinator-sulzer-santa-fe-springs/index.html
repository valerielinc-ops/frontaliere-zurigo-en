Role overview

Sulzer is a leading engineering company with a proud heritage of innovation.

Acts as the liaison between the customer, sales, vendors, engineering, and production departments, determines and provides detailed cost summaries and quotations for repair jobs in the service center.

Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities
  • Reviews customer requirement for pump repair
  • Analyzes inspection results, works with shop personnel and Area Superintendent to determine scope of work required for repairs
  • Plans repairs (labor hours, purchase requirements, work instructions, and total cost) Generates repair quotes
  • Completes disassembly, inspection, repair, and assembly work instruction packages, (work orders, prints, procedures, inspection forms).
  • Make/ review sketches and drawings to ISO standard and in AutoCAD where required.
  • Completes purchase requisitions.
  • Consults with vendors to determine availability, quality, quantity, and best pricing of all materials relative to a given job.
  • Evaluates supplier quotations and determines acceptable quality, quantity, and pricing requirements to meet commitments made to customers.
  • When necessary, expedite parts and materials from suppliers to ensure delivery commitments are met.
