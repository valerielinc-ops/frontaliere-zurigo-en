Main responsibilities

  • Main tasks and responsibilities
  • Act as a subject matter expert and manage engineering requests associated to tendering and project execution for Turbo-machinery Services in the region.
  • Conduct site equipment inspection, root cause analysis and technical forums to support sales team to identify repairs, upgrades and retrofit opportunities
  • Challenge traditional Sulzer thinking to ensure we remain ahead of the competition without taking excessive risk
  • Review Technical specifications, provide comments/exceptions, input Technical know-how as required to ensure proposals are technically fit for purpose
  • Identify and document areas of technical risk and level of design review associated with Projects at an early Tender stage
  • Liaise with oversea Sulzer competence centers, specialist functions (e.g.
  • Aero, welding, rotor-dynamic, C&I, etc) and key sub-contractors to clarify client requirements and ensure technical aspects have been adequately addressed
  • Consolidate technical offer and associated technical documentation (e.g. data sheets, calculations, technical bid assessment questionnaires) as necessary to satisfy client's Tender requirements
  • Provide an Engineering presence, when required, on customer visits, for assessment, bid clarification, client kick off meetings, project execution etc
