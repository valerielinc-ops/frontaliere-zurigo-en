  • Review Technical specifications, provide comments/exceptions, input Technical know-how as required to ensure proposals are technically fit for purpose

  • Consolidate technical offer and associated technical documentation (e.g. data sheets, calculations, technical bid assessment questionnaires) as necessary to satisfy client's Tender requirements
  • Provide an Engineering presence, when required, on customer visits, for assessment, bid clarification, client kick off meetings, project execution etc
