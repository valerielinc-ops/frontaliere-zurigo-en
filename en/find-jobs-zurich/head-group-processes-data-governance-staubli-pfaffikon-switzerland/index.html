Role overview

The Head Group Processes & Data governance is responsible for defining, governing, and continuously improving end-to-end business process landscape and master data governance across at Group level.

The role ensures clear standards, governance mechanisms, and accountability structures are in place, and orchestrates the Group Process Owner network to drive alignment, efficiency, and data integrity across divisions.
