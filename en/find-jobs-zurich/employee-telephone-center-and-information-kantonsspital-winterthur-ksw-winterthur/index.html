Role overview

For the telephone control center and information department, which comprises around 12 employees, we are looking for a committed personality who strengthens our team immediately or by agreement.

We connect external and internal callers with hospitalised patients, secretariats, office centres, departments and staff.

We provide information about the hospital location and the areas in the telephone exchange and at the information, opening and visiting times, the car park, existing appointments, emergency operations and much more.
