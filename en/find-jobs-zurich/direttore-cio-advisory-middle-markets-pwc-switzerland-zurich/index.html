Role overview

PwC Consulting has a strong foundation of clients in the middle market and family business sectors.

In addition to serving large multinational corporations, we aim to further strengthen our presence in the second and third tier client segments.

To support this goal, we are seeking a Director / Partner specializing in Technology Strategy & Transformation / CIO Advisory .

  • PwC Consulting has a strong foundation of clients in the middle market and family business sectors.
  • In addition to serving large multinational corporations, we aim to further strengthen our presence in the second and third tier client segments.

Company and context

  • Develop creative, high-quality solutions to current challenges of our clients in the key areas of technology strategy, organization, operating-model, assessment and other related topics.
  • Actively drive the acquisition of new projects and clients.
  • Be involved in the further development of the service offering for Technology Strategy & Transformation Advisory.
  • Work in an inspiring team and an international network, receive regular feedback from colleagues and superiors and have access to first-class training in the areas of technical know-how and soft skills.
  • Take responsibility for employees in the role of coach and accompany your coachees in their professional and career development.
  • Successful career in consulting and excellent methodological and technical skills.
  • Scientific and/or technical background or in economics and experience in leading and supporting strategic technology or transformation projects.
  • Able to quickly grasp complex scenarios and interrelationships and address them with solution-oriented and entrepreneurial thinking.
  • Proven expertise in common methodologies and frameworks (TOGAF, COBIT, SAFe, IPMA, PRINCE, SCRUM, etc.).
  • Interest in the further development of the team and the expansion of the service offering and show initiative to shape them both.
