Role overview

Modern embedded systems, sophisticated real-time applications and innovative technologies – in this versatile function you contribute significantly to the development of powerful software solutions.

Together with a dedicated team, you accompany software projects from conception to successful commissioning and take on a high degree of personal responsibility.

Your tasks in detail

  • Development of software for real-time systems on powerful embedded platforms based on Xilinx Zynq 7000 and Ultrascale SoCs
  • Self-responsible processing and implementation of software projects throughout the entire development process
  • Commissioning and integration of software in the overall system as well as ensuring a reliable function
  • Further development and maintenance of internal libraries to support a sustainable software architecture
  • Conduct code reviews and actively participate in regular reviews within the development team
  • Close cooperation with internal departments and continuous improvement of development processes WHAT YOU SHOULD TEACH
  • Completed studies (ETH/FH) in computer science, electrical engineering or a comparable field of study
  • Several years of experience in embedded software development as well as in-depth knowledge in the mentioned areas of responsibility
  • Very good user knowledge in C
  • Knowledge of C++ and/or FreeRTOS is advantageous
  • Experience with Qt and/or Vitis DIE as well as with functional safety according to IEC 61508 standard desirable
  • Very good knowledge of German and English in word and writing
  • High degree of self-motivation and pleasure in independently advancing topics and taking on responsibility
  • Independent, pragmatic and quality-oriented working methods as well as strong teamwork skills WHAT WE ARE TO YOU
  • A dynamic working environment with exciting projects
  • Flexible working hours (active and trusted working hours)
  • Very good social benefits and a super-compulsory pension fund
  • Employee benefits of a large corporation, employee share purchase program
  • Promotion of needs-based training
  • Staff restaurant and external catering and shopping facilities nearby
  • Individual internal and external personnel development measures in cooperation with our Rheinmetall Academy
  • Workplace a few minutes walk from the Zurich-Oerlikon train station KONTAKTDETAILS Responsible contact: Michael Zmoos For questions about your application, please use the contact form.
