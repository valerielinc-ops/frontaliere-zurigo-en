Role overview

Shape the data-driven future of the Fresh Food & Beverage Group.

You combine business understanding with modern BI technology and contribute directly to data-based corporate management.

In the BI team, you will develop sustainable reporting and analytics solutions at the interface of supply chain, finance, planning and with Fabric and Power BI.

  • Shape the data-driven future of the Fresh Food & Beverage Group.
  • You combine business understanding with modern BI technology and contribute directly to data-based corporate management.
  • You will analyze requirements from areas such as Finance or Integrated Business Planning (IBP) and translate them into reporting and analysis solutions.
  • Together with the stakeholders, you accompany data and BI projects from definition to implementation.

Main responsibilities

  • You will analyze requirements from areas such as Finance or Integrated Business Planning (IBP) and translate them into reporting and analysis solutions.
  • Together with the stakeholders, you accompany data and BI projects from definition to implementation.
  • You develop and optimize data models, SQL views and data flows in Microsoft Fabric (Warehouse, Lakehouse, Dataflows).
  • You create and manage modern reporting solutions in Power BI including semantic models, Power Query and DAX.
  • You take responsibility for the independent design and implementation of BI initiatives and actively shape the further development of our reporting and analytics landscape.
  • You support the expansion of BI governance, reporting standards and a future-proof "AI-ready" BI platform. Requirements
  • at least 3 years: in a controlling environment or a comparable environment
  • Completed studies (uni/ETH, FH, HF): Business Informatics, Data Science, Business Administration or a similar discipline
  • Understanding business processes and key performance indicators, ideally in the areas of supply chain operations (SCO) or finance.
  • Ability to transform business requirements into sustainable BI and analytics solutions.
