Role overview

Canton of Zurich Directorate of Justice and Home Affairs Imprisonment and Reintegration Medical Service Doctor / Medical Service 50% Imprisonment and Reintegration (JuWe) is responsible as part of the Directorate of Justice and Home Affairs of the Canton of Zurich for the execution of penalties and measures as well as the execution of various types of detention.

For our 1,400 employees from around 40 professions, the successful social reintegration of people who have committed crimes and the prevention of recidivism are of particular importance.

The newly created Main Department of the Medical Service provides basic medical and somatic care for the detained persons in all prison institutions of the Canton of Zurich.
