Role overview

SOMOSA offers an inpatient 6- to 12-month intensive care program for 14- to 18-year-old clients who are in a severe adolescent crisis.

The aim of the treatment is to stabilize the clients in their personality to such an extent that they can survive in the existing supporting or replacing socialization systems when they leave.

As a recognized Listespital in the field of child and adolescent psychiatry and as a youth home with a socio-educational mission, we fulfill both provisions through a long-standing, intersystemic cooperation in the fields of social pedagogy, care, psychotherapy and work pedagogy.
