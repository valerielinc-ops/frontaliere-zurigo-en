Company and context

  • You lead, promote and develop your dialysis team professionally and organizationally and create a motivating working environment.
  • You ensure high-quality, safe and patient-oriented care as well as compliance with our quality standards.
  • You plan and coordinate the personnel deployment and ensure that the dialysis station runs smoothly.
  • You are actively involved in day-to-day care and perform complex dialysis-specific treatments and shunt punctures professionally.
  • You promote close interdisciplinary cooperation and are committed to the continuous development of processes and quality standards.

  • You have very good knowledge of German in word and writing, further language skills are an advantage.
