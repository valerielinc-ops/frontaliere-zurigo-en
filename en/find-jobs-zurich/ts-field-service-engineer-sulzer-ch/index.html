Main responsibilities

  • Main tasks and responsibilities
  • Pre-commissioning and commissioning of installed, repaired or overhauled rotating equipment.
  • If required supervise and assist with the DIRA of rotating equipment and associated auxiliary equipment according to technical drawings and specifications.
  • Systematic trouble shooting, root cause analysis and rectification on mechanical, electrical and instrumentation component and systems with consideration on quality, time and cost.
  • Lead for start up and testing of new installations/erections or overhauled units of rotating equipment including mechanical part, electrical part, instruments and control system
  • Monitor field sequence activities based on agreed scope and schedule and report regularly to office.
  • Ensure that all findings and work is clearly reported in writing.
  • Work assignment could be at shop or field service depends on customer projects request
  • Communicate with customer or third party on site as RES representative
  • Ensures that all works and activities are done in line with the ISO procedures, as well as the HSE and company regulations.
