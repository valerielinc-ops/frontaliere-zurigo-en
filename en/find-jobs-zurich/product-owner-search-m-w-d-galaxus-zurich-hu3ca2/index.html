Role overview

As Product Owner – Search, you own the vision and delivery of one of Digitec Galaxus's most impactful user-facing surfaces.

Working at the intersection of data, relevance, and UX, you translate search intelligence into measurable improvements for millions of shoppers – with high autonomy in a flat, trust-based engineering culture where outcomes matter more than output.

Main responsibilities

  • Own the product roadmap for your team's domain, from discovery and prioritization through to delivery and measurement
  • Define, prioritize, and maintain a well-structured backlog in close collaboration with the Team Lead, designers, and engineers
  • Lead product discovery - facilitate workshops, user research syncs, and cross-team alignment to shape initiative direction before committing to solutions
  • Partner with stakeholders across Search, UX, Data Science, and other product areas to align on goals and manage expectations
  • Track and interpret product KPIs (e.g.
  • CTR, exit rate, conversion) to drive data-informed decisions and evaluate initiative outcomes
  • Collaborate with engineers as peers - providing strategic direction while trusting the team's technical judgment and avoiding micromanagement
  • Identify and challenge architectural or process constraints that limit the product's ability to scale or improve
  • Keep stakeholders informed with clear, transparent communication - shielding the team from noise while maintaining appropriate visibility
  • Actively contribute to team culture, methodology improvements, and knowledge-sharing within the broader PO community

Key requirements

  • 3+ Jahre: Search & Relevance / E-Commerce Search Experience
  • Abgeschlossenes Studium (Uni/ETH, FH, HF): Product Management (Digital / Tech)
  • 3+ years of experience as a Product Owner, Product Manager or in a similar digital product role Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Business Informatics, Information Systems or a related field, or equivalent practical experience
  • Experience working in agile product teams and managing product backlogs
  • Solid understanding of search technologies, including relevance, ranking, indexing, filtering and query processing
  • Experience using product analytics, interpreting A/B test results and making data-driven product decisions
  • Familiarity with APIs and the ability to discuss technical concepts and trade-offs with engineers
  • Knowledge of ML/AI-powered search, including vector search, hybrid search, embedding models and LLM-based retrieval, is a strong plus
  • Experience designing or optimizing search experiences, including filters, product listings and instant search, is an advantage
  • Strong ownership, collaboration and communication skills Fluent in English; German is a plus
  • The capacity to adapt to ongoing changes in the work environment
  • Werte statt blabla: Kooperativ, innovativ, piratisch, eigenverantwortlich, ambitioniert – diese fünf Werte fordern wir alle und leben wir alle.
