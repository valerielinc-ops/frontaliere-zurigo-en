Main responsibilities

  • You pay particular attention to the freshness of fruits, vegetables and bread and declare war on food waste with far-sighted orders
  • Order fresh products and of course all other goods, accept them, check them and fill them on the shelves in your store
  • Correctly implement requirements in the store layout
  • Check best-before dates in the freshness product group
  • As a deputy, instruct, encourage and advance the employees - and be a role model for them
  • As a deputy, take joint responsibility for the numbers - in sales, Personnel costs and inventory requirements
  • Three or more years of professional experience: in the retail trade as a specialist, department or district manager would be an advantage
  • Completed vocational training (EBA): in the retail trade (food) or a related professional category would be ideal
  • Enjoyment of fresh products and ideally already professional experience with them
  • Physically fit, some colis weigh up to 10 kilos
