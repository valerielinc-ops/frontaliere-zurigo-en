Role overview

As a Guest Relations Manager (GRM), you can prove your high level of service awareness in dealing with our inpatients with supplementary insurance on a daily basis.

You act as a central contact between our patients and the various clinics at the USZ.

The University Hospital Zurich is one of the largest hospitals in Switzerland.

  • As a Guest Relations Manager (GRM), you can prove your high level of service awareness in dealing with our inpatients with supplementary insurance on a daily basis.
  • You act as a central contact between our patients and the various clinics at the USZ.
  • You are the contact person for non-medical needs of privately and semi-privately insured patients with regard to their concerns, wishes and feedback.
  • In addition, you will look after additional insured persons before, during and after their stay at the USZ (including contact, welcome, farewell, care at the bed station, escort and luggage transport in our house).
