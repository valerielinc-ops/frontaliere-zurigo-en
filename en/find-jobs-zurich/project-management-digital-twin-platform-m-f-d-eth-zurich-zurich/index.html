Role overview

Project management Digital Twin Platform (m/f/d) 70%-100%, Zurich, temporary Printed Printing ETH Zurich is developing the future-oriented, digital basis for the planning, construction and operation of its infrastructure and building portfolio in the Digital Twin programme.
