Role overview

Tasks: The field of activity includes the treatment of alcohol- and drug-dependent patients in individual and group setting and the support in solving their social problems.

Supervisions and clinic-internal training events provide an opportunity to deepen daily experience and knowledge.

They take over the co-management and later the independent management of group therapies, are used for various representation situations in individual and group setting and will take over case management under supervision.

They are introduced to documentation and reporting in order to be able to do this work independently later.

The area of activity also includes the diagnostic investigation and the support of the scientific evaluation of the therapy offers.

Requirements For this position, we expect a recently completed university degree with a major in psychology or a ZHAW master’s degree with a specialization in clinical psychology.

We would like to see a specialist with an interest in interdisciplinary cooperation in a team of doctors, psychologists, design, sports and occupational therapists with the professional goal of the Swiss-approved psychotherapist.

We offer: With this position limited to 15 months, we offer you responsible tasks in a motivated, modern and well-connected environment.

Your professional and personal development is a concern for us, which we support generously and specifically with structured further education and training offers.

In addition, you receive weekly supervision, which can be credited depending on the training institute and status.
