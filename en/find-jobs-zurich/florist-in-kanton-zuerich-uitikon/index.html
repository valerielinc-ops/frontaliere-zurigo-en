Kanton Zürich Justizvollzug and Wiedereingliederung (JuWe) Description

The Kanton Zürich Justizvollzug and Wiedereingliederung (JuWe) is part of the Justice and Interior Department of the Canton of Zurich and employs around 1'400 women and men with diverse professional profiles.

The JuWe is responsible for the criminal justice system and the various forms of detention.
