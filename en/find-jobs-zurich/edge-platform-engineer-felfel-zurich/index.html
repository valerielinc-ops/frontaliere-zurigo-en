Key requirements

  • Solid background with roughly 5+ years of experience building and operating production systems, specifically managing a real fleet of devices with live users, outages, and OTA updates.
  • Proficiency in at least two of the following languages: C#, Go, or Kotlin, coupled with a willingness to consolidate and standardize our tech stack.
  • Working knowledge of edge platform networking (VPNs, NAT, flaky links, office networks), running Linux and Android on small hardware, OS-level debugging, and observability for offline-tolerant systems.
  • Strong ability to treat hardware as an interface, including practical experience with Modbus, USB, serial connections, reading sensor datasheets, and working with vendor SDKs.
  • Familiarity with tools like Balena, Azure IoT Hub, KubeEdge, Kubernetes, Docker, or experience in commercial appliance ecosystems is a strong plus.
  • Pragmatic problem-solving mindset with a track record of putting the problem before the programming language, preferring standard, reliable tools over inventing custom solutions. Fluency in English, German is nice to have

Application process

  • Take full, end-to-end ownership of the edge runtime on our smart fridges and Gavetti coffee machine controllers, managing everything from zero-touch provisioning, device bootstrap, call-home and on-device applications.
  • Drive the consolidation of our fleet management onto a single, reliable platform (such as Azure IoT Hub or KubeEdge), taking charge of making and defending the architectural choices.
  • Streamline the technology stack by reducing the number of languages running on the edge, bringing the device world closer to our cloud environment.
  • Collaborate closely with and report directly to the Head of Engineering to specify the next generation of hardware, select vendors, and define software contracts.
