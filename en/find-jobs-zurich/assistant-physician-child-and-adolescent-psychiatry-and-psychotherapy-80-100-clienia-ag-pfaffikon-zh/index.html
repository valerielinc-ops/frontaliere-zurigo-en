Role overview

You take over the treatment of adolescents aged 12-18 years of both sexes with disorders from the entire child and adolescent psychiatric spectrum (including ADHD, depression, suicidal crises, self-harming behavior, anxiety disorders, eating disorders, personality disorders, etc.).

In addition to crisis treatment, diagnostic investigations and longer-term therapies are carried out.

You come from Switzerland or the EU and are interested in working with children, young people and their parents.

  • You take over the treatment of adolescents aged 12-18 years of both sexes with disorders from the entire child and adolescent psychiatric spectrum (including ADHD, depression, suicidal crises, self-harming behavior, anxiety disorders, eating disorders, personality disorders, etc.).
  • In addition to crisis treatment, diagnostic investigations and longer-term therapies are carried out.
