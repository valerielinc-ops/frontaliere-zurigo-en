Application process

  • You will work on groundbreaking projects that transform advancements in simulation into real-world applications, making a significant impact on both our technology and the world. What you'll be doing:
  • Lead the forefront of Physical AI progress in both simulated and real humanoid robots by building the humanoid reference platform that features NVIDIA's robotics technologies.
  • Work together with various groups to seamlessly incorporate NVIDIA robotics products like Jetson Thor, Isaac Sim/Lab, Isaac ROS into all-encompassing solutions for humanoid robots.
  • Work on making things function seamlessly on real robots in our Zurich lab, focusing on training and deploying whole-body controllers, manipulation policies (VLA/WAM) and embedded agents (CaP). What we need to see:
  • 5 years of experience in Computer Science, Robotics, Engineering, or a related field, with proven experience in the industry.
  • Proficiency in C++ and Python programming languages and experience applying them to real-time robotics systems.
  • Hands-on experience with both simulated and physical robots.
  • In-depth knowledge of tools and libraries for robotics and learning, including PyTorch, ROS2, and physics simulators like Isaac Sim or Newton.
