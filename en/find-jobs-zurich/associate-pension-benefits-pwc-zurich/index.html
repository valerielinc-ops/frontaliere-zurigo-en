Role overview

You will be part of a multi-disciplinary consultancy team with a strong actuarial and quantitative focus, supporting pension consulting in Switzerland.

The team advises national and international corporate clients as well as pension funds on the design, valuation and transformation of occupational pension schemes.

A key focus lies on actuarial modelling, financial analysis and regulatory-compliant valuation work.

  • You will be part of a multi-disciplinary consultancy team with a strong actuarial and quantitative focus, supporting pension consulting in Switzerland.
  • The team advises national and international corporate clients as well as pension funds on the design, valuation and transformation of occupational pension schemes.
  • Depending on expertise and interest, support technical work such as actuarial pension valuations under IFRS or US GAAP, audits where pension specialist input is required, quantitative analyses, pension benefit calculations, impact assessments of plan changes and, benchmarking studies.
