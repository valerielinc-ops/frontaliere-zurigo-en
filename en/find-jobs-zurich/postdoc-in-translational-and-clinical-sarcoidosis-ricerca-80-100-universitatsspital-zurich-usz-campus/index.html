Role overview

This postdoctoral position focuses on immunological and data driven research in sarcoidosis within our Sarcoidosis Center of Excellence.

The successful candidate will contribute to the development and evaluation of novel diagnostic tools, the assessment of emerging therapeutic approaches and the identification of biomarkers and predictive models to improve patient stratification and clinical outcomes.

The project builds on our ongoing research involving a large, well-characterized cohort of patients with sarcoidosis, with the overarching goal of advancing personalized disease management and developing tools for predicting disease course and treatment outcomes.
