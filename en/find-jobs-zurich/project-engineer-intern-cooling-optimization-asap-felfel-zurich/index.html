NICE TO MEET YOU! IN SHORT Are you a hands-on engineering student who enjoys a mix of deep analytical work and getting out of the office to solve real-world problems? As our new Project Engineer Intern (ASAP, 2-3 months, 100%) based in Zurich Wiedikon, you will be right in the middle of the action. Balancing your time between our office and on-site field visits, you will investigate root causes and optimize the performance and reliability of our smart hardware fleet across Switzerland. After all, FELFEL is about good food. • Valid work permit or Swiss passport. YOU WILL BE MEASURED BY
