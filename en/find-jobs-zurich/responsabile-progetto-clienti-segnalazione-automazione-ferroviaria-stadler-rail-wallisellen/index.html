Description

PROFILE

  • You have the passion for the realization of very innovative and fascinating signaling and railway automation projects
  • You have completed higher technical education (university, university of applied sciences, HF), ideally with further business management
  • You have several years of experience in managing customer projects, ideally in the area of signaling technology, or other relevant project experience in the railway industry
  • You know the current developments in the area of automation technology in the railway industry
  • You are not only good at communicating in German, but also in English and/or French (level C1 in spoken and written)
  • You enjoy business trips (< 10% travel) TASKS
  • You have overall responsibility for the handling of complex customer projects in the area of signaling/railway automation (CBTC, ATO/C-DAS, ESTW)
  • You ensure that projects are carried out on time, within budget and in accordance with the contract
  • You are in charge of the Customer support and communication with partners, suppliers, authorities and other stakeholders
  • You lead and coordinate an interdisciplinary team from all areas along the value chain
  • You represent the customer project internally to management and motivate your project members sustainably
  • You develop innovative solutions together with the team to meet technical and non-technical customer requirements
