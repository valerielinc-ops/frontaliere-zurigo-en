Role overview

For our quality department in Zurich, we are looking for an SAP Q module key user to strengthen and actively support our teams.

They are responsible for the support and data maintenance of the SAP Q module and train and support employees in the quality department.

With active participation, you are also responsible for the development and verification of data bases for the continuous improvement and further development of the Q module.
