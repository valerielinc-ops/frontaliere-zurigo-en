Main responsibilities

  • Job Description In this project-related role you will take on the following tasks and responsibilities:
  • Project management Planning: Overall responsibility for the operational control, monitoring and coordination of all planning phases of the construction project Living Lab HIL.
  • Overall provider communication: Lead management, negotiation management and structured exchange with the external overall provider and the specialist planners.
  • Interface management: Central coordination between ETH Real Estate, the overall provider, the team of teaching and research integration and the planning team.
  • Participation Management: Close cooperation with the management, taking on operational management tasks and representing project interests in strategic committees.
  • Risk and quality management: Ensuring the overall project goals in terms of deadlines, costs, sustainability targets and qualities in the complex context of research and construction practice.
  • The position is limited to a maximum of five years, initially with a one-year contract with extension options.
  • Profile To meet the requirements of this demanding position ideally, you have the following profile:
  • Education: Master's degree in architecture (ETH/FH or comparable).
  • Professional experience: Well-founded, long-standing experience in project management and overall control of complex large-scale construction projects in Switzerland.

Application process

  • The aim is to further develop sustainable design and construction in the existing building and to apply it in an exemplar way to a real planning and construction project.
