Canton of Zurich Directorate of Justice and Interior Prisons and Reintegration Zurich Social worker in Winterthur Prison 60

80% Prison and Reintegration (JuWe) is responsible for the execution of penalties and measures as well as the implementation of the various types of detention as part of the Directorate of Justice and Interior of the Canton of Zurich.

For our 1,400 employees from around 40 professions, the successful social reintegration of people who have committed crimes and the prevention of recidivism are of particular importance.
