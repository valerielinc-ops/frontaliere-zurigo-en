Role overview

In short

We are looking for a visionary and technically proficient Head of Product Development | to lead our accessories product engine.

This is a critical leadership role where you will be responsible for translating creative concepts into highly functional, durable, and commercially viable (technical) performance and lifestyle accessories.

  • In short
  • We are looking for a visionary and technically proficient Head of Product Development | to lead our accessories product engine.
  • Key Responsibilities
  • Execute overall accessories development strategy and map out a roadmap which aligns with the overall brand, product, and financial goals

Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities
  • Execute overall accessories development strategy and map out a roadmap which aligns with the overall brand, product, and financial goals
  • Oversee all development and ensure commercialization of the product range from ideation to launch, including development engineering, sampling, testing, milestone management, and final product specifications
  • Work as a partner to Design, Materials, Innovation, Product Management, and Sourcing teams to ensure successful delivery of seasonal products and execute strategic vision for the business
  • Deliver against gross margin targets and manage department budgets while optimizing on costs
  • Conduct comprehensive market research to identify emerging trends, competitive threats, and new market opportunities
  • Support and partner with our Sourcing teams in Asia & Europe on building a robust global sourcing strategy that emphasises high-quality products, world-class partnerships, service, ethical sourcing and sustainability
  • Implement supply chain initiatives, in partnership with our ESG team, that reduce environmental impact, such as using recycled and preferred materials, advanced water treatment and color dyeing technologies and optimising logistics with initiatives like localisation
  • Stay at the forefront of emerging technologies and market trends to identify and oversee the development of innovative accessories that meet performance, durability, and sustainability requirements, which will instigate new ways of thinking about products
  • Establish and enforce rigorous product testing and quality control protocols to ensure compliance with industry standards and brand requirements

Additional details

  • This role will report into the Global Senior Director of Product Creation + Sourcing.
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with key suppliers, engage with industry stakeholders, including athletes and sports professionals and work with the cross-functional stakeholders to gather customer feedback and incorporate it into future product designs.
  • Familiarity with the latest creation software and technologies relevant to accessory design, development and production.
