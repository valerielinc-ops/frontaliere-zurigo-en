Original description

What you should know about us The Emil Frey Group is not only a Swiss family company with over 100 years of experience, but also one of the leading companies in the automotive industry. Quality, tradition, innovation and customer orientation are the main pillars of the company. The focus of the activities is on the import of car brands, the operation of garages, the logistics of spare and accessories as well as the provision of financial services. Emil Frey Digital AG is part of the Emil Frey Group and is a full-service communications service provider for the automotive industry and responsible for digitalization within the Emil Frey Group. We are building the AI division at Emil Frey on Baue with us the AI division in the Emil Frey Group. As a newly formed team, we shape the AI strategy and operating model, develop governance standards together with security, legal, IT and data and support the business areas in making AI measurable in business. From the business case to risk analysis to the evaluation of partners and technical solutions. We manage Switzerland’s AI portfolio, lead projects, build AI agents and automated workflows. Internationally, we ensure transparency about the initiatives of the national subsidiaries so that synergies are used throughout the Group. As part of the AI team, you will help build the new area, make decisions, take responsibility for your own projects and shape how AI works at Emil Frey. If you have a hands-on mentality, technical understanding in the AI field, structured approach and a pragmatic way of thinking, we look forward to your application as: Product Owner AI Portfolio & Delivery Your tasks • Operational operation of the intake gate: inclusion, completeness and plausibility check of incoming AI initiatives (Epic), return to the business units with specific questions • Plans and maintains the CH AI backlog and prioritizes initiatives based on the given prioritization logic • Implementation & coordination of risk classification
