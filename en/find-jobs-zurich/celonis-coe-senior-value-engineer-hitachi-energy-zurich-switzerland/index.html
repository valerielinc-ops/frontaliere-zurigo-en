Role overview

The Opportunity In our data‑driven organization, we rely on accurate, timely, and high‑quality process insights to improve operational performance and enable informed decision‑making.

As a Celonis CoE Senior Value Engineer (Data & AI Focus), you will play a key role in enabling these insights by designing and maintaining robust data pipelines, modeling process data, and integrating information from core business systems into the Celonis platform.

Additionally, you will help industrialize AI-driven process intelligence, enabling predictive insights, automation, and decision support across SCM and other domains—reducing dependency on manual analysis and accelerating value realization.

How You'll Make an Impact Data Pipeline Engineering: Design, build, and maintain reliable ETL pipelines to extract, transform, and load data from ERP, CRM, and other enterprise systems into Celonis.

Data Modeling: Develop optimized data models to reflect end‑to‑end business processes, enabling advanced process mining, root‑cause analysis, and AI-driven insights.

AI Enablement of Process Intelligence: Enable AI\/ML use cases (e.g., prediction, anomaly detection, root cause analysis) by preparing high-quality datasets, features, and data structures to support advanced analytics.

Integration Ownership: Integrate data from SAP, Oracle, databases, APIs, and external sources, ensuring consistent data availability for both analytics and AI use cases.

AI & Automation Integration: Support implementation of AI-powered capabilities within Celonis (EMS, action flows, predictive analytics, AI copilots), embedding intelligence into workflows and decision-making.

Performance Optimization: Continuously improve performance of Celonis data models and pipelines to support scalability for analytics and AI workloads.

Data Quality for AI: Establish data validation, enrichment, and cleansing frameworks to ensure reliability of both reporting and AI-driven outputs.

Application process

  • Understanding of AI\/ML concepts including predictive analytics, anomaly detection, classification models, and advanced analytics applications.
  • Experience preparing and managing data for AI use cases, including data quality, feature engineering, and model integration into business processes .
  • Ability to translate complex technical, data, and AI concepts into actionable business insights while collaborating effectively across IT, data science, and business teams.
  • Passion for Process Intelligence, Automation, and AI-driven transformation , combined with a strong ownership mindset, continuous learning approach, and excellent English communication skills. More
