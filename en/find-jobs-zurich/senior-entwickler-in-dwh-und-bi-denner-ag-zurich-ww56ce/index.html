Role overview

We are looking for an experienced developer for our team "DWH and BI" to help build a new data warehouse.

Together with the specialist departments, you shape Denner’s data-driven future and work in a dynamic, motivated team.

  • We are looking for an experienced developer for our team "DWH and BI" to help build a new data warehouse.

Main responsibilities

  • Build a new retail DWH based on a modern tech stack (Azure, Fabric) and actively shape data architecture
  • Focus on building the core DWH and working closely with other teams on data integration and reports

  • Integration of forward-looking technologies and strategic impulses for the further development of the data warehouse Requirements
  • Several years of professional experience: in the area of DWH and BI based on Microsoft products (Azure & PowerBI)
  • Completed studies (uni/ETH, FH, HF): in the field of computer science or equivalent training
  • Experience in Data Warehouse (DWH) and Business Intelligence (BI), ideally with a focus on retail
  • Experience in designing and aligning a DWH/BI solution, including collaboration with Data Science/AI
  • Experience in DevOps best practices with responsibility for development and operation, ideally for data platforms
