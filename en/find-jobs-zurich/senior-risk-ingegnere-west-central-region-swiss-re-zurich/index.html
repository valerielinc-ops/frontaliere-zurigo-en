Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities
  • Act as a Strategic Advisor to underwriting and our clients shifting beyond transactional assessments to create insight-driven decisions, stronger and deeper client partnerships and lasting value
  • Lead account servicing for multinational and domestic large and medium-sized corporate clients as part of their property insurance programs, serving as the primary technical point of contact
  • Support Swiss Re's West Central Region general property portfolio, driving continued growth across direct insurance, primary lead business, and international business — both for bound and new business
  • Review, interpret, and summarize risk assessments conducted by Swiss Re Corporate Solutions risk engineers, service partners, and third-party insurance risk engineers, ensuring accuracy and actionable insights
  • Collaborate with and advise underwriters on risk quality and exposure for existing accounts and new business opportunities, enabling preferred risk selection
  • Perform full account engineering functions including developing customer service plans, negotiating fees, coordinating field servicing, managing service partners, and engaging with underwriters, brokers, and clients
  • Leverage Risk Engineering data analytics tools to conduct portfolio analysis and benchmarking, develop account stewardship materials, provide value-added customer support, and drive meaningful risk improvement efforts
  • Conduct on-site risk assessments and operations reviews periodically to verify risk mitigation approaches and provide customers with practical, impactful, risk insights and risk improvement advice
  • Preparation of external loss control and internal underwriting reports , as well as developing project loss control specifications, and performing project plan review
