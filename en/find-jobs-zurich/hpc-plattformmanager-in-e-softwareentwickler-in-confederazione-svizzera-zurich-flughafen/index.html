Role overview

  • Ensure the use of the HPC platform (CSCS) for MeteoSwiss applications and drive their further development together with internal and external partners
  • Accept, prioritize and represent requirements from operations, research and development to the CSCS and coordinate improvements in performance, stability and scalability

  • Several years of practical experience in software engineering, preferably with Python and modern software development practices as well as cloud-native architectures
  • Experience in the operation and further development of high-performance computing environments as well as complex data and software workflows

  • Good knowledge of at least two official languages and of English
