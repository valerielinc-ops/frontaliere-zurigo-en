Role overview

The Psychiatric University Hospital Zurich treats and cares for mentally ill people of all ages.

The more than 2,500 employees of the 55 different professions make their contribution to the holistic care of patients in interdisciplinary teams, from clarification and therapy to follow-up care.

Our work is based on mutual trust and personal responsibility and together we promote an innovative corporate culture.

Application process

  • Application of basic knowledge in social work (theory-practice transfer)
  • Acquisition of skills in consulting and basic knowledge of selected diagnoses
  • Insight into a wide range of specialist areas of psychiatry and opportunities for internship in other professional groups
  • Open and appreciative working atmosphere in the interprofessional team
  • Discount shopping as well as sports offers, subsidized staff restaurant, free parking, etc.

Company and context
