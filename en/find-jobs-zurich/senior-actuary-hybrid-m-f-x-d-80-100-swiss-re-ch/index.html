Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities:
  • Act as Chief Actuary for one or more Continental European markets, providing technical leadership and oversight of pricing decisions, referrals and key actuarial assumptions
  • Monitor portfolio performance continuously — identifying emerging opportunities and risks, and supporting the design and execution of value-enhancing management actions
  • Partner cross-functionally with Client Management, Underwriting, Valuation and Portfolio Management teams to identify and unlock opportunities to enhance portfolio value
  • Influence strategic decisions by acting as a trusted counterpart to Inforce Optimisation and Portfolio Management teams — bringing constructive challenge and sharp actuarial insight
  • Lead actuarial assessments supporting repricing initiatives, recaptures, in-force transactions and other high-impact strategic projects
  • Contribute to client discussions, transaction structuring and negotiations, bringing both actuarial rigour and commercial perspective to the table
  • Support governance processes through referral reviews and independent actuarial challenge, ensuring robust decision-making frameworks
  • Connect the dots across the actuarial control cycle — building an end-to-end understanding of the business and feeding consistent insights back to the relevant teams
  • Represent your markets and contribute to selected Continental Europe cross-functional initiatives, broadening your impact beyond your core portfolio
