Role overview

As Senior Business Analyst you will be a key member of Avaloq’s Banking Operations division, acting as a functional lead within the Asset Data Sourcing team.

Banking Operations serves as the back office for more than 30 banks globally, ensuring efficient, compliant, and reliable processing across the Avaloq banking platform.

The Asset Data Sourcing team is responsible for the integration, mapping, governance, and distribution of financial instruments master data.
