Role overview

Tasks

  • A sympathetic cross-functional product team with hands-on team lead, product manager and several T-shaped full-stack engineers who share responsibility.
  • You work in variations of Scrum or Kanban. Engineers also roll up their sleeves in refinements and are actively involved in architectural and product issues.
  • A lot of design freedom and the opportunity to actively shape high code quality in the team and across team boundaries.
  • Enough legacy, complexity and grown structures to really make a difference with a good dose of resilience and to make systems step by step better.

Requirements

We match when you

  • Many years of profound experience in modern, product-oriented software engineering (preferably Java) and have already worked on complex, distributed systems - ideally in e-commerce or in comparable domains.
  • Modern engineering practices are not only theoretically familiar, but have lived in everyday life, for example in the areas of CI/CD, automated testing, decoupling, code reviews and observability.
  • See concepts like DDD, Clean Architecture, Event-Driven Architecture, SOLID, Feature Flags or Dependency Injection not as buzzwords, but as tools that you use meaningfully and pragmatically.
  • You are a strong advocate of “You build it, you own it” and have experience in environments where it is released several times a day.
  • Share your knowledge, enjoy pair programming, whiteboard sessions and code reviews, and help develop the engineering level in the team.
  • Bring depth in your area of expertise and at the same time move safely across adjacent areas.
