Role overview

Eraneos is an international strategy, transformation and technology consulting group, dedicated to empowering organizations to thrive in an ever-changing digital age. By bringing together top-tier experts from business and tech, we help clients to continuously raise the bar in successful transformations, from strategy to execution. Whether we’re designing future-ready organizations, unlocking the potential of data and AI, or securing businesses with cutting-edge cybersecurity, we deliver results pragmatically. Our team of around 1,200 dedicated professionals is based in offices across Switzerland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Austria, the UK, Singapore, and the USA, and seamlessly blends global perspectives with strong local roots. Around 450 employees work at our Swiss locations in Zurich, Bern and Lausanne.

  • Design and blueprint scalable Salesforce CRM architectures tailored to client needs and business goals.
  • Lead platform selection, integration, and implementation initiatives from an architectural and guiding perspective.
  • Analyse complex business and technical requirements and translate them into robust, future-proof Salesforce strategies.
  • Advise clients on best practices related to scalability, security, governance, and data management within the Salesforce ecosystem.
  • Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to ensure successful delivery, adoption, and continuous improvement of Salesforce solutions.
  • You are passionate about driving digital transformation in the pharmaceutical or broader life sciences industry.
  • You have proven experience as a CRM Architect or in a comparable senior CRM leadership role, ideally with a focus on Salesforce.
  • You combine deep technical expertise with strong business acumen and enjoy designing solutions that create real business value.
  • You communicate confidently in both German and English (spoken and written) and can convey complex concepts clearly and effectively.
