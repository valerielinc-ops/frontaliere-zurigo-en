Role overview

Eraneos is an international strategy, transformation and technology consulting group, dedicated to empowering organizations to thrive in an ever-changing digital age. By bringing together top-tier experts from business and tech, we help clients to continuously raise the bar in successful transformations, from strategy to execution. Whether we’re designing future-ready organizations, unlocking the potential of data and AI, or securing businesses with cutting-edge cybersecurity, we deliver results pragmatically. Our team of around 1,200 dedicated professionals is based in offices across Switzerland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Austria, the UK, Singapore, and the USA, and seamlessly blends global perspectives with strong local roots. Around 450 employees work at our Swiss locations in Zurich, Bern and Lausanne.

We are seeking a highly experienced and visionary Salesforce Architect with a Salesforce Certified Technical Architect (CTA) credential to join our growing Life Sciences consulting practice. In this role, you will lead the design and implementation of Salesforce-based solutions for global clients in the pharmaceutical, biotech, and medical device sectors. You will act as a trusted advisor, guiding clients through digital transformation journeys and aligning technology with business strategy.

  • Design scalable, secure, and high-performance Salesforce solutions tailored to the unique needs of Life Sciences clients.
  • Lead end-to-end architecture across Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Health Cloud, Experience Cloud, and custom applications.
  • Ensure architectural integrity and alignment with enterprise IT strategies and compliance requirements (e.g., GxP, HIPAA).
  • Engage with senior stakeholders to understand business goals and translate them into technical solutions.
  • Conduct technology assessments and provide strategic recommendations for Salesforce platform adoption and optimization.
  • Facilitate workshops, discovery sessions, and roadmap planning with cross-functional client teams.
  • Oversee the technical delivery of Salesforce implementations, ensuring best practices in development, integration, and deployment.
  • Support change management and user adoption strategies.
