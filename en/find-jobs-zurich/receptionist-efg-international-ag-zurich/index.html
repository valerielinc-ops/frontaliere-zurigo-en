JOB DESCRIPTION

The receptionist is the first point of contact and ensures a seamless, premium client experience in line with five‑star hospitality standards. They provide professional, client‑focused reception and switchboard services, while efficiently handling a variety of administrative tasks.

1. Hospitality services


2. Admin support
  • Follow the Hospitality service protocol (SOP).
  • Serves visitors by greeting, welcoming and directing them appropriately. Taking care of guests anticipating their hospitality needs and making the experience impeccable according to our corporate standard quality.
  • Physically welcome clients and visitors (Escort and seat clients in lounges, offer a drink, and serve).
  • Set up and restore lounges/conference rooms + service.
  • Maintains security and controlling access. Issuing visitor badges. Noticing and reporting suspicious activity.
  • Organise and book meeting rooms.
  • Support internal event and management meeting (e.g. organise, manage, and order breakfasts/lunches for meetings upon request.
  • Handle sensitive information in a confidential manner comply with all internal regulations.
  • Register external visitors and issue badges.
  • Keep statistics (number of clients/visitors).
