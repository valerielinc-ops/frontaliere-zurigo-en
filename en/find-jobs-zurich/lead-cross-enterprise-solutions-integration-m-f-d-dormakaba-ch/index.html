Role overview

Lead a cross‑functional team delivering innovative, integrated processes and solutions across Data, Analytics and HR -enhancing efficiency, effectiveness and value while reducing complexity enterprise‑wide; drive process integration, process intelligence and automation as shared services for all Business Applications, ensuring alignment with strategic priorities, regional scalability, agile continuous improvement, and user‑centric outcomes that keep dormakaba competitive, data‑driven, and future‑ready. In addition, the role ensures enterprise‑level data quality & governance, architecture alignment and cost‑efficient designs, and safe, predictable releases across applications and platforms. The Lead Cross‑Enterprise Solutions & Integration owns and orchestrates enterprise services across:

  • Data Architecture & Governance / MDM including enterprise standards, lifecycle rules, quality, migration.
  • Analytics & BI including global reporting architecture, consistency, enablement, and lifecycle delivery.
  • HR Solutions including design, build and run of HR landscape with compliant enhancements and rollouts.
  • Solution Architecture (Business Applications) including full lifecycle - from early assessment and conceptual architecture to governance, compliance, and implementation guidance - across all relevant business applications.
  • Process Integration & Design and Automation.
  • Release & Change Management: synchronized enterprise release planning/execution, CAB governance, readiness, and post‑release validation. The IT Release Manager collaborates with application owners, development teams, infrastructure teams, business stakeholders, and external partners to manage the full release lifecycle—from release planning and dependency mapping to deployment, communication, readiness checks, and post‑release validation. This position plays a central role in ensuring cross‑application alignment, improving release stability, and strengthening governance across the enterprise.
