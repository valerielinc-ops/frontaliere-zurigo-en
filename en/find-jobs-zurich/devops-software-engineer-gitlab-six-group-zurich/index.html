  • We are looking for a DevOps Engineer to maintain and evolve our GitLab platform and enhance our GitLab components with related tooling, that empower product teams across the organization to increase efficacy and code quality. What You Will Do

  • Proficiency with CI/CD concepts: branching strategies, pipeline as code, artifacts, environments, approvals, and compliance controls
