Company and context

  • To strengthen our commitment to delivering exceptional product quality and customer experience, we are looking for a Director Reliability Engineering to lead our reliability strategy, build organizational capability, and drive reliability excellence across the full product lifecycle.
  • In this highly visible leadership role, you will combine people leadership, technical problem-solving, and strategic product reliability planning.
  • You will lead a team of reliability engineers, partner with stakeholders across R&D, Quality, Operations and Product Management, and ensure reliability is embedded into both current products and future innovations. More about the role
  • Lead, develop, and structure a high-performing Reliability Engineering team, establishing effective operating models, governance, and best practices.
  • Drive complex product reliability investigations, root cause analyses, and failure assessments of field returns and customer complaints, translating findings into actionable improvement plans.
  • Partner with key customers and internal stakeholders to communicate technical findings, present recommendations, and feed reliability learnings back into product development.
  • Define and execute Sonova’s reliability engineering strategy, ensuring Design for Reliability principles, methods, and decision-making are embedded from concept through design, verification, industrialization, and lifecycle management.
  • Establish and continuously improve reliability methodologies, including D-FMEA, Critical-to-Quality frameworks, reliability planning & accelerated life testing, and data-driven failure analysis.
  • Identify reliability risks and opportunities within the innovation pipeline, proactively assessing future products and technologies before market launch.
  • Lead cross-functional improvement initiatives that enhance product robustness, customer satisfaction, and long-term business performance.
