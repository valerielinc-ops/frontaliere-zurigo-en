Helvetic Warranty GmbH is an independent Swiss company in the field of embedded insurance and market leader in Switzerland.

Company and context

  • You will oversee topics in the operation and in the further development of our IT landscape and support operational, organizational and technological projects within our IT organization.
  • You work closely with the business, your IT colleagues and external partners and ensure an efficient and transparent flow of information.
  • You recognize connections, prioritize tasks independently and make well-founded decisions to promote topics efficiently.
