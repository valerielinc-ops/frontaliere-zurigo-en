The Senior Pump Mechanic is responsible for the inspection, disassembly, repair, reassembly, balancing, and testing of industrial pumps and rotating equipment in a high-quality service center environment.

Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities
  • Perform complete teardown, inspection, and rebuild of centrifugal pumps, multistage pumps, vertical pumps, and other rotating equipment
  • Diagnose mechanical issues including wear, imbalance, misalignment, and hydraulic performance problems
  • Balance individual components such as impellers as well as fully assembled rotors to meet OEM and industry standards
  • Assemble rotating elements including shafts, sleeves, bearings, and impellers with proper fits and tolerances Conduct hydrostatic testing on pump cases
  • Measure and record tolerances using precision tools (micrometers, calipers, dial indicators, etc.)
  • Interpret blueprints, technical drawings, and OEM specifications
  • Restore pumps to OEM or engineered specifications
  • Maintain a clean, organized, and safe work environment
  • Follow all company safety policies, procedures, and regulatory requirements
