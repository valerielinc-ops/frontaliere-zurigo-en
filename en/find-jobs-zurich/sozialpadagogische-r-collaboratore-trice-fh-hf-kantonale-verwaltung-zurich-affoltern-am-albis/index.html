Role overview

Canton of Zurich Directorate of Justice and Home Affairs Prison Imprisonment and Reintegration Prison Institutions Canton of Zurich Social Education/Employee (FH/HF) 80

100% Prison Imprisonment and Reintegration (JuWe) is responsible as part of the Directorate of Justice and Home Affairs of the Canton of Zurich for the execution of penalties and measures as well as the execution of various types of detention.

For our 1,400 employees from around 40 professions, the successful social reintegration of people who have committed crimes, the prevention of recidivism and the detention and preparation of the departure of persons without a valid residence permit are of particular importance.
