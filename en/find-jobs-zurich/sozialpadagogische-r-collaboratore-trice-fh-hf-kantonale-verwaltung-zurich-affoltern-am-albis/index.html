Role overview

Canton of Zurich Directorate of Justice and Home Affairs Judicial Enforcement and Reintegration Enforcement Institutions Canton of Zurich Social Education Staff (FH/HF) 80

100% Judicial Enforcement and Reintegration (JuWe) is responsible for the execution of criminal and measures as part of the Directorate of Justice and the Interior of the Canton of Zurich and for the implementation of various types of detention.

For our 1,400 employees from around 40 professions, the successful social reintegration of people who have become criminals, the prevention of relapses, and the detention and preparation of the departure of persons without valid residence permits are of particular importance.
