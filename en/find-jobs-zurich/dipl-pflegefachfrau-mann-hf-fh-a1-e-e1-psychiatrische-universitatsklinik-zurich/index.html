Role overview

The Psychiatric University Hospital Zurich treats and cares for mentally ill people of all ages.

The more than 2,500 employees from 55 different professional profiles work in interdisciplinary teams to contribute to the holistic care of patients, from assessment to therapy to aftercare.

Our work is based on mutual trust and personal responsibility and together we promote an innovative corporate culture.

Application process

  • Modular application of elements of DBT and cognitive behavioral therapy
  • Designing and implementing the daily structure and leisure activities
  • Supporting the vocational trainers the training of learners and students on the ward
  • Completed, recognized vocational training in the field of nursing HF/FH, (proof of recognition from the Swiss Red Cross for foreign diplomas)
  • High technical, social and self-competence and a high level of resilience as well as flexibility and analytical thinking
  • Very good knowledge of spoken and written German
  • High motivation and ability to get involved in an exciting and demanding work environment
  • Willingness to perform all duties including night and weekend shifts
