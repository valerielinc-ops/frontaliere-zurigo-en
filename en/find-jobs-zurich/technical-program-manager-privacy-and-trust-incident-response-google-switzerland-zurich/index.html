Main responsibilities

  • Organize and lead response teams for privacy or user trust incidents always with the utmost respect for our users’ data.
  • Lead the full lifecycle of complex incident communications, from drafting communications strategies to delivering communications.
  • Collaborate actively with engineering towards improved product processes and related policy decisions to prevent incidents and on education initiatives to improve incident response readiness.
  • Support automation, analysis and tool improvement initiatives in support of reduced time to resolution for all incident types.
  • Lead initiatives to improve our incident management or compliance processes, lead prevention initiatives to mitigate any risks related to privacy, security and user trust.
  • Be available for oncall to support our global team operations.

Key requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in a technical field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Experience working within privacy, incident management, or cybersecurity domains.
  • Experience driving QA, privacy, or security improvements at the project or team level.
