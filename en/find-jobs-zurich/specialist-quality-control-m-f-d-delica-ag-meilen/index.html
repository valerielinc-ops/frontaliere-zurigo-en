Role overview

You want to make the quality of food visible and enjoy it when everything is right in the laboratory – from analysis to approval?

In this role, you contribute directly to product safety and the enjoyment of our customers with precise laboratory tests and well-founded evaluations.

And best of all: While others only talk about taste, you know exactly what is really in it.

  • You want to make the quality of food visible and enjoy it when everything is right in the laboratory – from analysis to approval?
  • In this role, you contribute directly to product safety and the enjoyment of our customers with precise laboratory tests and well-founded evaluations.
  • You perform chemical-physical, microbiological and sensory analyses, evaluate the results and document everything cleanly in SAP.
  • You process quality deviations from laboratory tests, receipt of goods and production and initiate appropriate measures.

Main responsibilities

  • You perform chemical-physical, microbiological and sensory analyses, evaluate the results and document everything cleanly in SAP.
  • You process quality deviations from laboratory tests, receipt of goods and production and initiate appropriate measures.
  • You are responsible for managing, monitoring and maintaining the test equipment in the laboratory.
  • You evaluate raw, semi- and packaging materials in terms of shelf life and possible shelf life extensions.
  • You represent quality control in projects. Requirements
  • First professional experience: in the quality assurance of the food industry.
  • Completed vocational apprenticeship (EFZ): e.g. microbiology laboratory, chemical laboratory, food technologist, ideally with further training (e.g. dipl. food technician, HF, CAS, dipl. quality manager).
  • Precise, careful and reliable work with a high sense of responsibility.
  • Independent, flexible and communicative personality, capable of teamwork and decision-making.
  • Good knowledge of MS Office and SAP S/4HANA, ideally in the QM module. in German (negotiation certain) English (advantageous) Cosa of Friamo
