Role overview

Application for interns / intern Psychiatry (Focus Age and Adult Psychiatry) ## Duration and working conditions

  • Internship from 6 to 12 months (80–100% use)
  • Spital Affoltern AG – Affoltern am Albis
  • Employees by agreement --- ##STR1## Would you like to gain first experience in the psychiatric workspace and get a versatile insight into the care?

In the Centre for Psychiatry and Psychotherapy of the Affoltern hospital, you will be given the opportunity to actively participate in the everyday clinic and to find out if the nursing profession fits you.

The adult psychiatry comprises 24 beds and treats people aged 18 to 65 years.

The age psychiatry with 16 beds supervises people from the age of 65.

Our patients are in an acute mental crisis.

The focus is on the diagnosis, therapy and stabilization of mental diseases.

They accompany patients of adult and age psychiatrics through everyday clinics and support them in the daily structure.

They help the care team in simple nursing and organizational activities.

They support the department in logistical tasks and ensure that materials and work areas are organised.

You will get exciting insights into the diagnosis and therapy of mental illnesses.
