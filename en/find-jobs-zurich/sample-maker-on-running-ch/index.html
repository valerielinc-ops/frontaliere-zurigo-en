Sample Maker Job Summary

We are looking for a motivated and detail-oriented Sample Maker with a passion for athletic and ready to wear apparel.

In this role, you will be responsible for creating high-quality prototypes, muslins and mockups in partnership with pattern engineers, product development and designers to bring product concepts to life.

Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities:
  • Construct prototypes based on technical specifications, patterns, and alternative construction methods in collaboration with cross functional teams.
  • Determine optimal fabrics, trims, and sewing techniques for high-performance apparel.
  • Proactively identify and resolve construction challenges.
  • Support prototype-to-production transitions at a premium level.
  • Ensure samples meet strict quality standards for stitching, durability, and finish.
  • Maintain an organized workspace and efficient workflow.
  • Stay current on both athletic and ready to wear industry trends and advanced sewing techniques, specific to appearance and function.
