Role overview

Tasks

At Klinik Lengg we work in a large interdisciplinary team of almost 70 therapists with in-depth knowledge in various neurological disorders and treatment concepts. The occupational therapy team consists of 16 occupational therapists and 1 activation therapist. We also train students and attach great importance to interprofessional training and team meetings and offer plenty of space for professional exchange and individual promotion in a collegial environment. Through our close interdisciplinary and interprofessional cooperation, continuous development is possible.
