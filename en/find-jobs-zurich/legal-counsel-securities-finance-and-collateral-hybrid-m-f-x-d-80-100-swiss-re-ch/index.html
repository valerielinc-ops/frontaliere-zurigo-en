Role overview

Are you a sharp, commercially minded legal professional with deep expertise in financial markets and collateral?

This is your opportunity to take on a high-impact role at the heart of Swiss Re's Capital Markets Legal function — advising on complex, multi-jurisdictional matters and shaping the legal framework for one of the world's leading reinsurance groups.

As Legal Counsel for Securities Finance and Collateral, you will be a trusted legal advisor embedded in a dynamic and internationally connected team.

Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities
  • Manage day-to-day legal matters related to collateral trading and management, including the drafting and negotiation of master agreements for Securities Financing Transactions (SFTs), triparty collateral agreements, trade confirmations, security agreements, and bespoke collateral arrangements
  • Advise on financial markets regulations , with a particular focus on the Swiss regulatory landscape, ensuring Swiss Re remains compliant with evolving legal and regulatory requirements
  • Conduct legal and regulatory risk gap assessments and provide timely, well-reasoned legal opinions on operational and legal risks
  • Draft and review legal policies and guidelines , ensuring they reflect current regulatory requirements and best practices
  • Collaborate closely with Compliance, Credit Risk, Finance, and Asset Management stakeholders to deliver integrated, commercially sound legal solutions
  • Engage with market associations such as ICMA and ISLA, representing Swiss Re's interests and staying ahead of industry developments
  • Provide periodic updates to Senior Management, Internal Audit, and relevant Committees on legal developments and risk matters
  • Coordinate with the governance team for regulatory filings and reporting obligations
  • Escalate material issues appropriately to the Head of Global Collateral and the Head of Capital Markets, Finance – Legal
