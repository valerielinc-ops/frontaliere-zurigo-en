Main responsibilities

  • As an Azure Data Engineer , your main responsibilities will involve:
  • Design and implement the target data model for risk accumulation, covering both location-based catastrophe exposure and non-location-based lines, including full exposure history.
  • Architect and develop data ingestion ETL pipelines to migrate data from legacy accumulation data sources into a modern Databricks based platform.
  • Integrate underwriting pipeline data (submissions and quotations) into accumulation reports, ensuring current and in-progress risk views are available to stakeholders.
  • Build and optimize long-running batch jobs for data transformation, aggregation, and snapshot management using Databricks and Delta Lake.
  • Establish data quality frameworks, automated validation checks, and monitoring for all ingested and transformed datasets.
  • Collaborate with and provide backup to the Data Engineer and API Developer on the team, ensuring no key-person dependencies exist across the platform.
  • Work in close collaboration with the wider technology and business teams to deliver an excellent, reliable data platform. Your Skills and Experience
