Role overview

Sulzer is a leading engineering company with a proud heritage of innovation.

Join our global team to grow your expertise and develop innovative solutions that enable a prosperous and more sustainable society. Job Summary

The Tendering Engineer is responsible for providing technical support to sales teams and conducting technical analysis of product configurations to determine customer-specific pricing.

  • Sulzer is a leading engineering company with a proud heritage of innovation.
  • Join our global team to grow your expertise and develop innovative solutions that enable a prosperous and more sustainable society. Job Summary

Application process

  • This job profile is intended for early-career professionals who apply broad theoretical knowledge and require the ability to investigate and analyze problems.
  • Ensure application of Sulzer tools and policies, procedures, codes of conduct and best practice guidelines
  • Review inquiry documents/specifications and prepares quality quotations on time for Sulzer Pumps' products using current tendering tools & procedures independently or with guidance
  • Analyzing customer requirements, particularly of complex applications and products for relevant market / application
  • Independent development of suitable technical solutions with the involvement of specialist departments
  • Consulting customers and the sales team on possible pump applications and in preparation of specifications, Configuration of pumps and accessories
  • Enquiry and evaluation of subcontractor offers as well as corresponding expediting
  • Co-operation with the plants in the Energy & Infrastructure Business Unit factory network
