Role overview

Eraneos is an international strategy, transformation and technology consulting group, dedicated to empowering organizations to thrive in an ever-changing digital age. By bringing together top-tier experts from business and tech, we help clients to continuously raise the bar in successful transformations, from strategy to execution. Whether we’re designing future-ready organizations, unlocking the potential of data and AI, or securing businesses with cutting-edge cybersecurity, we deliver results pragmatically. Our team of around 1,200 dedicated professionals is based in offices across Switzerland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Austria, the UK, Singapore, and the USA, and seamlessly blends global perspectives with strong local roots. Around 450 employees work at our Swiss locations in Zurich, Bern and Lausanne.

  • Digital Transformation Leadership: Drive the digital transformation of our clients by identifying and implementing innovative digital solutions in R&D, with a focus on Data&AI, Clinical, or Pharmacovigilance.
  • Project Management and Team Leadership: Lead and manage multidisciplinary project teams, ensuring the successful delivery of high-quality consulting services. Foster a collaborative, digitally-savvy team environment that promotes innovation and continuous improvement.
  • Digital Tools Adoption: Support clients in adopting and integrating advanced digital tools and platforms, ensuring seamless implementation and maximal business impact.
  • Strategic Advisory on Digital Transformation: Provide strategic guidance on digital transformation initiatives, helping clients develop and implement comprehensive digital strategies aligned with regulatory and pharmacovigilance objectives. Support them through their digital transformation journey, ensuring smooth integration of new technologies and processes.
  • 5+ years of experience leading transformative system implementations in the respective area (CTMS, eTMF, RIM, QMS, CDMS, PV), preferably Veeva Vault – or equivalent transformation initiatives with substantial business impact.
  • Proven experience in designing and implementing Data&AI use cases in R&D .
  • 5+ years of consulting experience in a management role, including driving sales and business development by leveraging a strong client network within Europe.
  • A passion for shaping the Industry within Eraneos and for our clients, ultimately delivering value for patients.
