Role overview

Do you want to contribute to Zurich's Global Investment Management organization, its business strategy, projects and to pursue innovation and continuous improvement?

Investment Management is Zurich’s center of competence for managing over USD 160bn of insurance assets.

This role combines senior derivatives investment expertise with end‑to‑end implementation ownership, quantitative tooling leadership, and strong governance accountability.

It sits at the intersection of strategy, execution, risk, and operations, requiring both deep technical skills and the ability to drive cross‑functional alignment in a regulated insurance asset management environment.

What you will do Derivatives Implementation, Infrastructure & Monitoring - Assist the Head of Financial Engineering in the management of team mandates, ensuring effective utilization of resources and adherence to strategic goals - Own the end‑to‑end implementation of derivatives programmes across local, regional levels, from structuring and pricing through execution, monitoring, and lifecycle management.

  • Design, enhance, and maintain derivatives pricing, analytics tools, and structured payoffs, ensuring robustness, documentation, and calibration governance.
  • Lead the development of market and risk monitoring dashboards, providing timely and actionable insights on rates, credit spreads, volatility, equity factors, and counterparty risk.

OTC Derivatives Operations & Collateral Management - Coordinate the full non‑cleared derivatives value chain, ensuring alignment between Legal, Middle Office, Treasury Operations, Risk, Custodian, and external counterparties.
