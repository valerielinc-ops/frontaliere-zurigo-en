Role overview

In this role, you will become the strategic architecture authority for Digital & Tech Asset Management (D&T AM), ensuring alignment with Swiss Re’s enterprise-wide architecture vision and business priorities.

You will act as a senior architectural leader, driving measurable improvements in platform resilience, engineering productivity and data-centric transformation while maintaining enterprise-grade security and cost discipline.

Working closely with the EA leadership team, Design Authority, domain architects, and senior business and IT stakeholders, you will ensure our platforms, data ecosystems, and integration capabilities evolve in a secure, scalable, and future-ready manner.
