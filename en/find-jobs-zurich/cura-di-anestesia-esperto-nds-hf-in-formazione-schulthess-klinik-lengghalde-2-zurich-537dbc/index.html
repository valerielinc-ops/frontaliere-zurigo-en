Role overview

Tasks

As an expert in anesthesia care NDS HF, you accompany patients before, during and after surgery. You work in a specialized clinical environment, monitor patients during anesthesia and contribute significantly to their safety and care.

In our orthopaedic clinic you can expect a varied further education with personal support, modern infrastructure and close cooperation in the interprofessional team.

What makes your education special with us

  • Schulthess Klinik offers the postgraduate degree program in cooperation with the Z-INA Higher School for Intensive, Emergency and Anesthesia Care in Zurich
  • Two of the total of four semesters are completed as part of our cooperation in the Zurich City Hospital (Triemli location)
  • The part-time course of study lasts 24 months (4 semesters) with a 100% period
  • You learn in alternation between theory and practice and are gradually introduced to complex anesthesiological situations

Requirements

This is what you should ideally bring

  • Diploma as a nurse HF or Bachelor of Science in Nursing FH
  • Foreign degree: Recognition by the Swiss Red Cross (SRC)
  • At least 12 months of professional experience in an acute care area of a hospital or clinic
