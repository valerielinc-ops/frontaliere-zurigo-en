Role overview

Join a dynamic team that prides itself on being true partners to a diverse clientele, including banking, insurance, multinationals, and smaller organizations. We focus on solving complex problems through impactful and transformational projects, both large and small. Our team is a unique blend of specialists and all-rounders, fostering a culture of mutual support and shared growth. We value collaboration, ensuring that everyone's contributions uplift team performance and enhance client outcomes. We offer an engaging work culture that promotes continuous learning and professional development, providing opportunities to work on diverse projects that challenge and expand your expertise.

  • Engage with clients to understand their risk technology needs and deliver tailored solutions.
  • Implement leading GRC platforms, ensuring they align with client-specific risk strategies and compliance requirements.
  • Assess technology risks across client environments and provide actionable insights for improvement.
  • Advise clients on integrating risk intelligence into their technology landscapes for optimized decision-making.

  • Representing and growing our risk technology presence in the market.

  • Deep understanding of Risk Management Frameworks including COSO, ISO 31000, NIST, and COBIT.
