Role overview

The canteen of the Pedagogical University of Zurich, located directly at Zurich Central Station, offers space for 340 guests and an additional 100 seats in the courtyard in summer.

In addition to the 650 foods, which are produced daily, numerous aperos, banquets and seminar meals with up to 500 people are carried out.

In addition, the ZHAW cafeteria located on the site as well as the gastronomy of the Business School Zurich and the Bistro Sihlhof are provided with an attractive catering offer.
