Main responsibilities

  • Develop estimates for overhauls of gas turbine components.
  • Generate repair quotations and work scopes using Dynamics 365(hours & material).
  • Inspection and repair work scope development, review, evaluation, and periodic adjustment.
  • Collaborate with the component engineering team and department supervisor to develop, refine, and optimize fixturing and tooling designs.
  • Reverse engineering and part design improvement.
  • Develop customer specific and driven scopes.
  • Evaluate inspection results for rotating and stationary gas turbine components (e.g., buckets, nozzles, shrouds, casings)
  • Creation of incoming and outgoing disposition reports ensuring technical accuracy.
  • Creation and development of inspection and repair forms, repair specifications, written process specification, and component drawings (2D/3D prints and models).
  • Work with department supervisor and shop technicians to ensure process, repair, and tooling viability.

Key requirements

  • The ideal candidate will possess the following:
  • Bachelor's degree in engineering, Sciences, or related field / equivalent experience and training.
  • Knowledge in rotating/stationary equipment repairs or similar machinery.
  • Proficiency in all MS office software; particularly with Excel and Project.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-paced shop environment.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
