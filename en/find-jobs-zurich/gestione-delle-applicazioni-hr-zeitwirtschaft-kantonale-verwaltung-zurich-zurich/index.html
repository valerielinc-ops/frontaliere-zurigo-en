Role overview

Canton of Zurich Financial Directorate Office of Informatics Application Manager/in HR Time Management 100% The Office of Informatics (AFI) is the IT competence centre of the Canton of Zurich.

It provides comprehensive services in the area of central IT infrastructure and canton-wide applications for the Directorates, authorities, legal administration and independent institutions.

Commissioned with the implementation of the new strategy Information and Communication Technology (ICT) supports and supports the Office of Informatics the Canton of Zurich into the digital world.

Time management is far more than coming and going.

Together, we ensure that the processes relating to working hours, absenteeism and expenses work reliably for thousands of employees.

At the same time, we are shaping the future of HR-IT in the canton of Zurich with new technologies and systems.

If you like technical challenges and like to help shape them, we look forward to your application.

Your tasks

  • Operation, further development and optimization of cantonal time management solutions
  • Analysis and implementation of technical requirements in technical solutions
  • Cooperation and sub-project management in exciting projects around SAP HCM, time management and HR systems
  • Consulting of the HR office and other cantonal positions on technical issues
  • Coordination of internal specialist offices and external partners
  • Cooperation in the further development of the HR system landscape in the context of digital transformation
  • Face this exciting challenge, grow with us and become part of our team in technical application responsibility for the time economy.

Your profile

  • Higher education in computer science, business information technology or comparable qualifications
  • Experience in application management or in the supervision of complex business applications
  • Knowledge in the SAP HCM environment, especially time economy, of advantage
  • Understanding for technical connections, interfaces and system integration
  • Experience in projects and joy in working with different stakeholders
  • Self-employed, structured and solution-oriented approach Our offer:
  • Responsible task with a high impact for the entire project Canton of Zurich
  • Exciting environment between computer science, HR and digitalization
  • Working to further develop one of Switzerland's largest HR system landscapes
  • Modern forms of work and attractive employment conditions
  • A team that shares knowledge, supports each other and maintains an open, honest and appreciative cooperation
  • A lot of expertise and short decision-making methods
