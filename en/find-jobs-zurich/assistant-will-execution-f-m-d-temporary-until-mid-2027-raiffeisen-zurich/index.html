Role overview

Raiffeisen Switzerland supports the Raiffeisen banks with a team of specialists in the holistic advice of our customers in the areas of financial planning, pensions, taxes and inheritances.

In the area of inheritance consulting/enforcement of will, we continue to build up capacities to provide our customers with professional support in the orderly transfer of assets to the next generation.

In order to strengthen our team based in Zurich Airport (The Circle) as well as the possibility to work partly in the home office, we are looking for a communicative and innovative personality as an assistant inheritance consultancy / execution of will (f/m/d) on a temporary basis until mid-2027 as of October 2026 or by agreement.

  • Raiffeisen Switzerland supports the Raiffeisen banks with a team of specialists in the holistic advice of our customers in the areas of financial planning, pensions, taxes and inheritances.
  • In the area of inheritance consulting/enforcement of will, we continue to build up capacities to provide our customers with professional support in the orderly transfer of assets to the next generation.
  • Processing of customer inquiries as well as inquiries from bank advisors in the area of inheritance advice / inheritance grants
