Role overview

The Stiftung Sozialwerk Pfarrer Sieber (SWS) is committed to helping the needy on the basis of the Gospel.

With around 200 employees and 250 volunteers, it operates several health and social care facilities in Zurich and the surrounding area and, in particular, offers help with homelessness, addiction and other associated health and social problems.

Our specialist hospital for social medicine and dependency diseases Sune-Egge offers inpatient and outpatient treatment of patients with combined somatic and psychiatric diseases.
