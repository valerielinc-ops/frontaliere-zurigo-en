Klinik Lengg AG in Zurich is the centre of excellence for epileptology and neurological rehabilitation.

It is our aim to provide patients with neurological diseases with the widest possible rehabilitation and to ensure an accurate diagnosis and sustainable treatment of patients with epilepsy. Around 300 employees are committed to humanely, competently, innovatively and reliably ensuring that our patients regain the greatest possible independence - and thus their quality of life. Become part of one of our interdisciplinary and interprofessional teams.
