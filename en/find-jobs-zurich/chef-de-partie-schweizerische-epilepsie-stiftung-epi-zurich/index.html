Role overview

The Swiss Epilepsy Foundation (EPI) is a multi-spart company.

It comprises the EPI WohnWerk, the EPI Hospital School, the Lengg High School, the Dapples gift, a seminar centre with a restaurant and the EPI Central Services.

In addition, the Swiss Epilepsy Foundation and another foundation form the sponsoring body of Klinik Lengg AG, a clinic for epileptology and neurorehabilitation.
