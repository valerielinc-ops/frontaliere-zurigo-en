Role overview

Location: Zurich Work Pattern Percentage: 80-100% Help our clients successfully manage global challenges As a Senior Consultant in the Tech M&A cohort of our Deal Transformation practice, you will support corporate and private equity clients in understanding the technology dimension of their most important transactions.

Technology has become one of the defining value drivers in M&A—it shapes deal theses, influences valuation, creates integration and separation complexity, and ultimately determines how value is protected and delivered after close.

With AI accelerating change across industries, you will help clients assess technology opportunities, risks and disruption while supporting informed deal decisions and long-term value creation. How you will make an impact

Application process

  • Technology due diligence : Assess technology landscapes, applications, infrastructure, cloud, data, cybersecurity, operating models and technology spend to identify transaction risks and opportunities.
  • Technology integrations and carve-outs : Support technology separation and integration planning, including Day 1 readiness, TSA considerations, target-state design and implementation planning.
  • Technology strategy and value creation : Analyse technology strategy, AI impact, operating model improvements and value creation opportunities across the transaction lifecycle.
  • Technology cost and synergy analysis : Support technology cost assessments, synergy analysis and investment planning while working closely with financial and commercial deal teams.
  • Client delivery : Translate complex technology findings into clear analyses and executive-ready recommendations while collaborating with multidisciplinary deal team Who we are looking for
  • Entrepreneurial mindset with a strong interest in technology, M&A and value creation; curiosity for emerging technologies.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving capabilities, with the ability to simplify complex technology topics.
  • Bachelor's or Master's degree from a leading university.
