Role

Motivated, passionate, hard worker and positive individual who continuously aims to make outstanding contributions and effect a difference from day 1.

Main Responsibilites

  • Execute all BRM controls on Investment Solutions area on a monthly respectively quarterly basis and ensures precise and proper documentation of results
  • Preparation of the IS RSC and IS BRM control report
  • Ensure that the identified control NOKs and related risk mitigating actions are timely remediated
  • Contribution on preparation of the annual IS risk and control self-assessment (RSCA) as well as other ad hoc assessment & reviews.
  • Work with the Regional and Global PB BRM to achieve a robust First Line of Defence in the Area
  • Report on the internal control environment and potentially arising risks or issues.
  • Collaborate with local Compliance teams, Ops Risk and auditors for any ad-hoc reviews
  • Key contact person a IS Area Level for IT, tools, trainings topics and issues
