Role overview

The model station SOMOSA is also a young psychiatric clinic and youth home, with over 30 years of expertise in the treatment of complex adolescence disorders.

Our offer includes 3, and from August 2025 4 stationary units for young people of both sexes (14-18 years) as well as a separate outpatient clinic for diagnosis and treatment of all psychiatric disorders (6-18 years).

Our success is based on intensive intersystemic cooperation in the fields of psychiatry/psychotherapy, social education and agogics.
