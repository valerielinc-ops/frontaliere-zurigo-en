Role overview

The commercial facility management (KFM) supports and advises all postal departments and third-party tenants in topics relating to commercial and contractual issues throughout Switzerland.

  • The commercial facility management (KFM) supports and advises all postal departments and third-party tenants in topics relating to commercial and contractual issues throughout Switzerland.

Company and context

  • In a decentralized organization we support our customers from the area search to the purchase and the support up to the end of the use.
  • With you our regional customers are comprehensively looked after! You Can Make That Do
  • You lead a team of 9 employees (real estate managers:inside) in terms of personnel, technical and business management and you continue to develop your organizational unit.
  • In your area (Zurich, Schaffhausen and Central Switzerland) you are responsible for comprehensive, high-quality and efficient management and ensure our Swiss standards.
  • You actively support the daily operational business and take over a small portfolio and more complex special cases in the area of property management.
  • For your properties/portfolio, you will take on active marketing, owner representation during clarifications, as well as state recording and the determination of maintenance and development and return measures.
  • You are in close contact with the renters, the neighbourhood and the authorities, supported in the search/take over of rental space and negotiated smaller rental contracts
  • You play a leading role in optimizing processes, tools and instruments and take part in special tasks. You bring this with you
  • You have several years of experience in the management of real estate with a focus on commercial real estate.
  • You have completed a higher level of education with a specialization in real estate (e.g. real estate manager:in, marketer:in, real estate evaluator:in).
