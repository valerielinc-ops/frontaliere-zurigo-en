Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities include
  • Select and / or design relevant forest protection projects in areas where cocoa expansion is proven to generate deforestation (12-20 projects) in order to generate lasting risk mitigation strategies, in line with the company’s “Forest Positive” Target and EUDR compliance strategy;
  • Oversee the implementation of our global agroforestry program in close collaboration with our origin teams and external partners.
  • Implement and further develop our implementation model and roadmap for agroforestry;
  • Regularly report progress on agroforestry and forest protection to internal stakeholders and support external reporting to customers and other stakeholders;
  • Support the further development of our climate-related customer offering in order to increase funding available to the agroforestry and forest protection programs.
  • This includes support to our customer-facing team during meetings and their preparation
  • Support the further development and execution of our wider Thriving Nature strategy: as member of the corporate TN team, help to shape our global strategy on climate and nature and support its execution also beyond agroforestry and forest protection About you
  • PhD or MSc degree in natural resource management, forestry or agronomy
  • 10 years’ experience in developing and implementing agroforestry and / or sustainable forest management programs (on-site presence required)
