Company and context

  • Do you want a task in which you can contribute your organizational talent and at the same time accompany people in a demanding life situation?
  • Then you are exactly right with us.
  • To strengthen our versatile team in the ZIO Zurich lake in Richterswil, we are looking for you as a medical assistant immediately or by appointment.
  • Medical assistant (100%) Your everyday work: You organize, accompany and support our office hours in a well-structured way - with a lot of heart
  • You ensured a warm and smooth patient reception and are the first point of contact for our patients, who are always in good hands with you

  • You perform administrative tasks and ensure clean documentation (reporting from dictates)

  • Knowledge of foreign languages is a welcome bonus You show high flexibility and resilience and are always service-oriented You are communicative, approach patients openly and always professionally You enjoy dealing with people and a high level of empathy Your personality: Friendly, empathetic and service-oriented Reliable and well-organized team capable and flexible Maintained appearance with positive charisma
