Role overview

As a Legal Counsel – Trade Sanctions, you will step into a newly created role in Group Legal as a key legal advisor to the business on sanctions-related matters.

As part of a collaborative, international team of lawyers, you will contribute your legal expertise to protect and enable the Group's business worldwide, shaping how Zurich navigates one of the most dynamic and geopolitically sensitive areas of international regulation.

You will support Group Legal in safeguarding our global operations against sanctions-related risks, by providing practical, cross-border legal advice, monitor rapidly evolving international sanctions regimes (EU, UK, US, CH), and supporting the design and deployment of risk-mitigation strategies.
