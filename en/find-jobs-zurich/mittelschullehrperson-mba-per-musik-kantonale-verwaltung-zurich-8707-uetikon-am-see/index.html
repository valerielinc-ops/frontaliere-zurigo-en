Role overview

Canton of Zurich Education Directorate Secondary and Vocational Education Office Secondary School Teacher mbA for Music 50% The Cantonal School Uetikon am See (KUE) is a young school in the development phase.

She leads a high school secondary level and five of the Zurich maturity profiles (old-language, mathematical-scientific, modern-language, economic-legal and philosophical-pedagogical-psychological).

The number of pupils is almost 800, that of teachers and members of the administration is around 120.
