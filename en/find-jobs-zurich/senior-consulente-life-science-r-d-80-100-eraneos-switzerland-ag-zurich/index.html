Role overview

Eraneos is an international strategy, transformation and technology consulting group, dedicated to empowering organizations to thrive in an ever-changing digital age. By bringing together top-tier experts from business and tech, we help clients to continuously raise the bar in successful transformations, from strategy to execution. Whether we’re designing future-ready organizations, unlocking the potential of data and AI, or securing businesses with cutting-edge cybersecurity, we deliver results pragmatically. Our team of around 1,200 dedicated professionals is based in offices across Switzerland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Austria, the UK, Singapore, and the USA, and seamlessly blends global perspectives with strong local roots. Around 450 employees work at our Swiss locations in Zurich, Bern and Lausanne.

  • Digital Transformation Leadership: Drive clients' digital transformation by identifying and implementing innovative R&D solutions, with a focus on Clinical and Pharmacovigilance.
  • Project Management & Team Leadership: Lead and manage multidisciplinary project teams, ensuring the successful delivery of high-quality consulting services. Foster a collaborative, digitally-driven team culture that promotes innovation and continuous improvement.
  • Digital Tools Adoption: Guide clients in adopting and integrating advanced digital tools and platforms, ensuring smooth implementation and maximizing value.
  • Advisory on Digital Strategy & Transformation: Provide strategic guidance on digital transformation initiatives, helping clients develop and implement comprehensive digital strategies aligned with regulatory and pharmacovigilance objectives. Support clients throughout their transformation journey, ensuring seamless integration of new technologies and processes.
  • Experience in System & Process Transformation: 3+ years of experience in implementing transformative system and business processes in areas such as CTMS, eTMF, RIM, QMS, CDMS and PV, preferably with Veeva Vault or equivalent high-impact transformation initiatives.
  • Expertise in Data & AI Use Cases: Proven experience in designing and implementing Data & AI Use Cases in R&D.
  • Life Science Consulting & Industry Network: 3+ years of consulting experience in Life Science with a strong industry network.
  • Outstanding Communication Skills: Excellent ability to convey complex ideas clearly and effectively.
  • Industry & Client Impact: Passion for shaping the Industry within Eraneos and delivering value for clients and patients.
