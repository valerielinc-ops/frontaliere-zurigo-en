Role overview

Apply Psychotherapist 80-100% Spital Affoltern AG - Affoltern at Albis employees by agreement Psychotherapy is part of the Center for Psychiatry and Psychotherapy with the departments inpatient adult psychiatry, old-age psychiatry, mother-child ward, the integrated day clinic and the psychiatric-psychotherapeutic outpatient.

In addition to these tasks, psychotherapy employees also work in the acute hospital in the departments of geriatrics, medicine and palliative care.

To strengthen our team in our psychiatric-psychotherapeutic outpatient we are looking for a committed psychotherapist.

Their tasks

  • Treatment and counselling of patients and their relatives at the Center for Psychiatry and Psychotherapy in the Outpatient Setting
  • Independent planning, implementation and documentation of therapies
  • Report preparation according to the requirements of the clients (ordering doctors, health insurance, daily allowance insurance, accident insurance, IV and others)
  • Interdisciplinary and interprofessional exchange and cooperation
  • Cooperation with allocators and institutions
  • Participation in supervision, intervisions, case meetings and team meetings
  • Moderation of internal training
  • With appropriate qualification and interest also management of supervision possible
  • Establishment and management of outpatient group therapies with interest and competence possible your profile
  • You are a benevolent, responsible and resilient personality with a good dose of humor
  • You enjoy working with patients of all age groups of adulthood
  • They appreciate the independent work in the outpatient setting but also the connection to a team and bring openness to different therapeutic directions.
  • You have a great interest in interdisciplinary and interprofessional cooperation
  • You have completed further training as a Swiss-approved psychotherapist or are about to graduate
  • Several years of professional experience are advantageous, if possible also in the ambulatory setting We offer
  • Very varied, interesting activity in our ambulatorium
  • Working in a very well-coordinated, supportive, open and creative team
  • Space for self-responsible, self-initiative work with options for co-creation
  • Training opportunities in the form of supervision, intervision and hospital-internal and external training
  • Family institution with appreciative work culture and flat hierarchies
  • Attractive employment conditions with good insurance services Our culture – Your benefits «Bi öis can mer no»: With us it is family and personal.

As part of the Affoltern hospital team, you will also benefit from attractive employment conditions and numerous Fringe Benefits: Jokertag, shopping benefits and two subscriptions for free admissions to the FORTYSEVEN Wellness Baths are just a few examples of our Fringe Benefits.
