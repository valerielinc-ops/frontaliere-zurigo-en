Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities:

  • Draft, review and negotiate a broad range of complex commercial agreements, including, joint venture arrangements, technology agreements, sales terms and conditions, and procurement and supply contracts
  • Advise on financing transactions, including bond issuances, syndicated and bilateral credit facilities, and provide legal support to the Treasury function on a wide range of financing-related matters
  • Oversee and coordinate litigation, arbitration and other dispute resolution matters across the Group, including the management of external counsel
