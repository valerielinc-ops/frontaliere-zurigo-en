Role overview

The Executive Assistant & Operations Officer for Public Sector Solutions provides dedicated executive support to the Head of Public Sector Solutions (PSS), ensuring the smooth running of a complex leadership agenda, stakeholder interactions and key engagement activities.

This Executive Assistant role supports the Head of PSS in a fast-paced, externally facing environment.

The successful candidate will act as a trusted partner, helping to manage priorities, coordinate activities and ensure the smooth execution of leadership commitments in an international setting.
