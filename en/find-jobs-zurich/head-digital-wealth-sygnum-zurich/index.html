Role overview

We are looking for a commercially minded and client-centric leader to shape the next phase of growth of Sygnum's digital wealth business.

This is an opportunity to take on a senior leadership role with meaningful impact, combining strategic, yet fully hands-on leadership, senior client engagement, business development and a strong instinct for emerging client needs and future offerings in a fast-evolving market.

You will work at the intersection of traditional finance and digital assets, leading teams, building trusted relationships and helping position Sygnum as a trusted partner in the future of digital wealth.

Main responsibilities

  • Your main responsibilities:
  • Provide strategic leadership and further develop Sygnum's digital wealth business across private clients, External Asset Managers & Multi-Family Offices (EAMs & MFOs) and Sygnum Spark, strengthening growth, scalability and the long-term client service model
  • Lead and manage the teams across relationship management and client servicing, with clear people leadership responsibility and ownership of the associated budget
  • Define and implement the strategy for the digital wealth business in close alignment with the Group CEO and the Group Executive Board
  • Own the performance of a substantial client portfolio with meaningful business volume and revenue contribution, while building and stewarding senior relationships across key wealth client segments, distribution partners and strategic ecosystem connections
  • Collaborate cross-functionally with Asset Management, trading, custody, lending and other internal stakeholders to deliver differentiated investment, banking and digital wealth solutions for target client segments
  • Shape the continued development and positioning of Sygnum's digital wealth offering, identifying evolving client needs and translating them into client propositions, commercial priorities and future growth opportunities
  • Ensure the quality of the private client books with respect to financial and non-financial risk profiles, margins and client satisfaction, in line with regulatory requirements and Sygnum's risk and compliance framework
  • Drive continuous improvement of onboarding, servicing and other key client processes together with Compliance, Operations and relevant business partners
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with internal and external stakeholders and represent Sygnum credibly in the broader wealth, digital asset and strategic partner ecosystem
