Role overview

The range of clinical services of the Institute of Anesthesiology and Perioperative Medicine (IFA) includes all anesthesia procedures for outpatient and inpatient surgical and diagnostic procedures, including comprehensive pre- and postoperative care.

Further central tasks are the treatment of acute and chronic pain, the shock room care of critically ill patients and the internal clinical resuscitation service.

In addition, the institute provides emergency medical specialists for preclinical ground and airborne emergency care as well as the intensive care service in close cooperation with Schutz & Rettung Zürich (SRZ) and the Swiss Rescue Air Guard (REGA).
