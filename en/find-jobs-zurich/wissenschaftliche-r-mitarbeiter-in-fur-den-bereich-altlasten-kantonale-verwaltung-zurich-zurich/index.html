Role overview

The Altlasten section with its 16 employees is responsible for monitoring the investigation, monitoring and rehabilitation of contaminated sites.

It ensures that waste sites are sanitized with high urgency as quickly as possible, that the contaminated construction waste is disposed of in accordance with the law and that the costs are distributed among the polluters. Your tasks

Administrative monitoring of investigations, renovations and construction projects at contaminated sites Supervision of complex cases of execution Consulting of the affected land owners
