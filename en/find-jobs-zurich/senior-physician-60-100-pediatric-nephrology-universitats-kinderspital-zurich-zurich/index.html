Role overview

You look after children and young people with kidney diseases in outpatient and inpatient settings at Switzerland's largest children's hospital.

Together with the multiprofessional nephro team, you will carry out all kidney replacement procedures (hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, continuous hemofiltration) and look after children and adolescents before/after a kidney transplant.

You will maintain close collaboration with other pediatric subspecialties, the intensive care unit, transplant surgery and mental health services.
