Role overview

You will join the Wealth Planning Team at Union Bancaire Privée (UBP) in Zurich, Switzerland.

In this role, you will serve clients and partner with Relationship Managers to ensure that wealth planning needs are appropriately addressed.

You will provide guidance on succession and legacy planning, tax suitability, mobility planning, and wealth structuring—based on each client’s specific circumstances—to create added value and mitigate risk, collaborating with Compliance on an on-demand basis to ensure alignment with KYC/AML requirements and internal policies.

Main responsibilities

  • Main responsibilities:
  • Proactive engagement with bankers to recognize and anticipate clients’ needs, and to identify, and capture business opportunities.
  • Provide and train Private Bankers with up-to-date, relevant technical information to understand impactful tax and legal developments and take proper action.
  • Identify, present and discuss appropriate and compliant wealth planning solutions and strategies to meet clients’ needs: deliver customized service and bespoke strategies.
  • Meet clients and prospects (including business travel with bankers)
  • Actively promote Wealth Planning services across the Bank via ad hoc presentations and publications on selected, high-impact themes; actively support tax and legal wealth-preservation objectives.

  • Integrate with the local team of Wealth Planners based in Zurich; collaborate across UBP Group; report to Head of Wealth Planning – Switzerland, based in Geneva.
