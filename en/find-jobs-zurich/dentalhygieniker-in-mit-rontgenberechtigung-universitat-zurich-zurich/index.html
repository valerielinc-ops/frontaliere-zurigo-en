  • Clinical and X-ray studies of patients with the emphasis on plaque and gums, changes in the mucous membrane and the dental hard substance, carious lesions, degree of inflammation of the gums, bone pockets.

  • Instrumental removal of dental coverings.
  • Polishing of fillings and removal of filling surpluses.
