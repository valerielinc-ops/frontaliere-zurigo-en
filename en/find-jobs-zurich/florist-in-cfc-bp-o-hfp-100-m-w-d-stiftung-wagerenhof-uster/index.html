Role overview

For our lively business with flower shop / gardening / farm shops and glass house café we are looking for you as a creative, motivated Florist/in EFZ, BP or HFP with professional experience (100%) Do you enjoy the training of learners, are you customer-oriented and like to take on responsibility in day-to-day organization and purchasing?

Do you also like working in an inclusive team with employees in need of support?

Every single person is at the center with their individual quality of life.

We do everything we can to ensure that our approximately 250 residents, 246 daycareers and 52 employees at protected workplaces in the Wagerenhof can enjoy the highest possible quality of life.

  • Collaboration and guidance of employees in need of support
