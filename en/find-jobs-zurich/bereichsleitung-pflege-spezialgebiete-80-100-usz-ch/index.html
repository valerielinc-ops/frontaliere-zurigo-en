Role overview

As Head of Care Specialties, you will actively develop care at USZ.

Together with your medical and business partners, you are responsible for the interprofessional management of a medical field.

Together with your team, you ensure an efficient and quality-oriented implementation of the USZ strategy.

  • As Head of Care Specialties, you will actively develop care at USZ.
  • Together with your medical and business partners, you are responsible for the interprofessional management of a medical field.
  • Leadership & Development: Direct management and development of a dedicated team of around 7 managers as well as indirect management of around 1200 employees in the entire area.
  • Responsibility & impact: overall responsibility for the strategic, human and economic development of the area as well as ensuring the agreed objectives.

Main responsibilities

  • Leadership & Development: Direct management and development of a dedicated team of around 7 managers as well as indirect management of around 1200 employees in the entire area.

Company and context

  • Design & Change: Active participation in transformation processes as well as in the further development of structures, processes and organizational forms. Requirements
