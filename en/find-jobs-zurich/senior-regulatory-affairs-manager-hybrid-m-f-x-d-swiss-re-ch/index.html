Role overview

Are you passionate about re-/insurance supervisory and regulatory topics?

We are looking for a Senior Regulatory Affairs Manager who takes a leading role in the team managing key supervisory processes and projects in collaboration with the relevant internal stakeholders, incl. senior management.

Main responsibilities

  • Your main tasks and responsibilities will include:
  • Manage the relationship with FINMA together with the team and assume responsibility for supervisory requests, filings and regulatory reports.
  • Lead or contribute to ad-hoc interdisciplinary teams related to projects, transactions and situations with a supervisory dimension.
  • Lead the preparation of regulatory interactions such as the FINMA Supervisory College meetings, FINMA on-site inspections, regular senior level meetings, and ad-hoc exchanges.
  • Contribute to and coordinate data calls, field testing exercises, or consultations.
  • Identify, assess and monitor key regulatory developments and risks.
  • Facilitate internal understanding of the impacts and subsequent preparation for upcoming regulatory requirements.
  • Maintain close contact and cooperation with key internal stakeholders across Business Units, Finance, Legal & Compliance, Risk Management and other relevant internal functions.
  • Support the preparation of briefing materials and presentations for members of Swiss Re's Executive Committee and/or Board of Directors.
  • Contribute to and coordinate internal reports and presentation.
