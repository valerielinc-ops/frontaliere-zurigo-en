Main responsibilities

  • As a Corporate M&A Analyst, you will support the Corporate Strategy and M&A team in a variety of strategic and operational tasks.
  • You will gain comprehensive insight into the full M&A lifecycle while engaging in collaboration across ABB's diverse business functions.
  • Support in implementing group-wide M&A governance model
  • Assist in the rollout of a bolt-on deal prioritization tool
  • Manage the deal opportunity pipeline and coordinate with Business Area/Division Strategy teams to identify acquisition targets and divestment opportunities
  • Track and report on M&A pipeline and deal economics; support implementation of a common platform for opportunity and deal status monitoring
  • Help provide visibility on functional M&A resource loading, availability, and allocation
  • Support Corporate M&A deal execution efforts and assist in preparing M&A-related presentations for internal and external stakeholders
  • Stay informed on M&A market trends, competitor activity, and regulatory developments Your
