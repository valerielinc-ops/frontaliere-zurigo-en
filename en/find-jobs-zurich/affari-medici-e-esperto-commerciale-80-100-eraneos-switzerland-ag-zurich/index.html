Role overview

Eraneos is an international strategy, transformation and technology consulting group, dedicated to empowering organizations to thrive in an ever-changing digital age. By bringing together top-tier experts from business and tech, we help clients to continuously raise the bar in successful transformations, from strategy to execution. Whether we’re designing future-ready organizations, unlocking the potential of data and AI, or securing businesses with cutting-edge cybersecurity, we deliver results pragmatically. Our team of around 1,200 dedicated professionals is based in offices across Switzerland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Austria, the UK, Singapore, and the USA, and seamlessly blends global perspectives with strong local roots. Around 450 employees work at our Swiss locations in Zurich, Bern and Lausanne.

  • Lead digital transformation initiatives that integrate Real-World Evidence (RWE) and advanced analytics to demonstrate product value and enhance patient outcomes.
  • Develop and execute cohesive omnichannel engagement strategies that align Medical Affairs and Commercial teams for a seamless personalized experience for Healthcare Professionals (HCPs).
  • Utilize AI and advanced analytics to generate actionable insights, personalize scientific and promotional content and optimize customer engagement across all digital channels.
  • Architect and oversee the evolution of digital ecosystems, including HCP and patient portals to drive superior customer experience (CX) and scientific exchange.
  • Drive launch excellence by creating agile, data-driven content strategies that effectively communicate the product's value proposition to payers, providers and patients.
  • You are passionate about digital transformation in the pharmaceutical or life sciences industry.
  • You are a highly experienced digital strategy and communications leader with a rich career in Life Sciences.
  • You have proven experience working with Medical Affairs and Commercial leaders across various therapeutic areas and with the relevant technologies.
  • You are fluent in English (oral and written).
