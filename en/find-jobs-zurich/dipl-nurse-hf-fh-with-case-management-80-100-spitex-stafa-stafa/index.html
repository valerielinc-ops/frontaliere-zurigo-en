Company and context

  • We have converted our organizational form to semi-autonomous teams, i.e. we are guided by the famous Buurtzorg model, but have adapted it to our needs.
  • In an interdisciplinary team, with case managers, FaGes and housekeeping and support staff at your side, it should be a new form of work that is contemporary and allows for the necessary flexibility, but also structure and continuity. You have the following qualifications:
  • Completed training for the dipl. Care professional HF/FH Ideally Spitex experience Strong professional and social skills
  • self-employed and responsible working practices valid category B ticket
  • Your tasks: As a case-leading employee, you control the care process from the first visit with the needs assessment according to interRAI until completion.
  • You are the interface for interprofessional cooperation with all involved entities, advise relatives and accompanying health professionals as well as the care team.
  • You look after our customers holistically and resource-oriented. We offer you:
  • a varied, responsible and independent task in an innovative organisation; flexible conditions of employment;
  • 5 weeks of holidays (from 50 years of age 6 weeks);
  • very good social benefits (e.g. pension fund contribution 60:40); A motivating and appreciative working environment.
