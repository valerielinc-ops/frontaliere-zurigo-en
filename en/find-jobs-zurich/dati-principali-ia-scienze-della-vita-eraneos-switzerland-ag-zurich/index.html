Role overview

Eraneos is an international strategy, transformation and technology consulting group, dedicated to empowering organizations to thrive in an ever-changing digital age. By bringing together top-tier experts from business and tech, we help clients to continuously raise the bar in successful transformations, from strategy to execution. Whether we’re designing future-ready organizations, unlocking the potential of data and AI, or securing businesses with cutting-edge cybersecurity, we deliver results pragmatically. Our team of around 1,200 dedicated professionals is based in offices across Switzerland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Austria, the UK, Singapore, and the USA, and seamlessly blends global perspectives with strong local roots. Around 450 employees work at our Swiss locations in Zurich, Bern and Lausanne.

  • Advising leading life sciences companies on strategic questions related to data, AI and transformation.
  • Developing data & AI strategies, target visions and roadmaps with a clear focus on business value and scalability.
  • Structuring complex challenges along the value chain, from Research & Development to Medical, Regulatory and Commercial.
  • Leading programs, workstreams and client teams with a focus on impact, quality and feasibility.
  • Translating technological possibilities into robust management decisions and prioritized courses of action.
  • Building trust-based relationships with senior stakeholders and actively contributing to proposal development and market presence.
  • Further developing the life sciences practice through thought leadership, business development and the expansion of strategic client relationships.
  • Several years of experience in consulting, industry or technology‑driven transformation environments with a focus on data, AI or analytics.
  • A solid understanding of the life sciences industry, ideally in Research, Clinical Development, Medical Affairs, Regulatory or Supply Chain.
