Original description

About us We enchant the world with chocolate! Lindt & Sprüngli (Switzerland) AG is a subsidiary of the global Lindt & Sprüngli Group with its headquarters and production facility in Kilchberg on Lake Zurich. In Altendorf we operate our own logistics center, while the Lindt Cocoa Center in Olten produces cocoa mass for our own needs as well as for other European production facilities. Throughout Switzerland we proudly operate 15 Lindt shops, including iconic locations such as the Jungfraujoch and the Swiss public transport company in Lucerne. Over 1000 employees from more than 50 nations contribute to our success. What you do with us: - Conceptual responsibility for business and product-specific processes relevant to VAT at home and abroad (including in the areas of domestic, retail, inter-company, global travel retail and international distribution companies) - preparation of VAT invoices in Switzerland and abroad (Switzerland, Belgium) and implementation of sales and pre-tax agreements - coordination with external VAT service providers with regard to the recovery of pre-tax payments from third countries - contact person for VAT checks (technical monitoring, data processing etc.) - determination of VAT rates for goods calculations - examination of electronic assessment orders and BAZG invoices - responsibility for the Provision of statistical indicators to authorities - Technical support for cross-departmental projects and processes (incl. Implementation in SAP) - Preparation of evaluations and statistics for Saharan financial statements (monthly and annual accounts) and cooperation with audits - Monitoring and further development of the VAT processes including maintenance and updating of process and ICS documentation When you fit in with us: - Commercial training with further training in financial and accounting (e.g. specialist card) - Multi-year experience in financial accounting with a focus on VAT - Very good SAP skills and a strong understanding of the process
