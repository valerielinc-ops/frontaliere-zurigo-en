Main responsibilities

  • Main responsibilities
  • Perform preventive and corrective diagnostics, repairs and maintenance on a range of industrial pumps and agitators.
  • Ensure the commissioning and installation of new equipment among customers.
  • Train customer operators in basic equipment use and maintenance.
  • Write accurate and detailed intervention reports.
  • Manage spare parts stocks required for interventions.
  • Provide first-level technical support to clients and the service team.

Key requirements

  • Ability to read and interpret technical diagrams and plans.

  • Excellent communication and customer service skills.
