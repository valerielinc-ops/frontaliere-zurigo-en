Role overview

Marché Restaurants Schweiz AG is one of the 10 Swiss companies with the highest turnover in the field of full-service system gastronomy and is the clear number 1 in motorway gastronomy with the brand "Marché".

Since 2014, Marché Restaurants Schweiz AG has been part of the Coop Group as an independent subsidiary.

With our brands and concepts – Marché, Zopf & Zöpfli, Burger King, Popeyes and Firehouse Subs – we are not only present on motorways or at Zurich Airport, but also at the largest Swiss railway stations in Zurich, Bern and Basel as well as in some popular shopping centers in German-speaking Switzerland.
