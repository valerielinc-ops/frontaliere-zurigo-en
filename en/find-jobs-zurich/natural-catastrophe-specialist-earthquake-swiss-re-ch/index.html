Role overview

Are you passionate about using cutting-edge science to better understand natural catastrophe risk?

As a Natural Catastrophe (Nat Cat) Specialist you will develop and improve models for quantifying risk associated with earthquakes and related geological hazards (landslides, tsunamis, volcanoes, etc).
