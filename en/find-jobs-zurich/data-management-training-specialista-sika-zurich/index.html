Role overview

The Data Management Trainings Specialist designs and establishes a structured communication and training concept that ensures consistent understanding, correct usage, and sustainable adoption of the Product Information Management (PIM) and PIM system across the organization.

That enables corporate and business users, data roles, and stakeholders to know what PIM is and work effectively and compliantly with the PIM system by providing clear communication, role‑specific training, and ongoing guidance aligned with PIM governance, data quality standards, and organizational change requirements.
