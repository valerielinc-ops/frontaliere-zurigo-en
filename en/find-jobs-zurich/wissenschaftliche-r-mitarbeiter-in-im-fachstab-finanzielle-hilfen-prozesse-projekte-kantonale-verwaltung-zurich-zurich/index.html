Role overview

Canton of Zurich Education Directorate Office for Youth and Vocational Advice Scientific staff member in the Financial Assistance – Processes & Projects 80% Good for life – this is our motto.

As part of the Education Directorate of the Canton of Zurich, the Office for Youth and Vocational Counselling (AJB) is responsible for the offers in the field of child and youth welfare as well as in vocational counselling.

Our work focuses on children, young people and families.
