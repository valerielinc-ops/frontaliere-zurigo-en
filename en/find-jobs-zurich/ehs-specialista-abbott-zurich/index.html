Role overview

Zurich, Switzerland | Thoratec Switzerland GmbH (Abbott)

At Thoratec Switzerland GmbH , part of Abbott’s medical device division, we design and manufacture life‑saving heart pump systems used by patients with advanced heart failure around the world.

Our products — including CentriMag and HeartMate 3 — are developed and produced at our Zurich site and support patients every day in critical care settings.
