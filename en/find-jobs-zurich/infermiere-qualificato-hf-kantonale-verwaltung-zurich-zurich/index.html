The newly created Department of Medical Services ensures basic medical and cosmetic care for the imprisoned in all prison institutions in the canton of Zurich.

As a central organisational unit, it ensures harmonised and high-quality health care according to the equivalence principle and works closely with the Psychiatric Psychological Service and the JuWe institutions.
