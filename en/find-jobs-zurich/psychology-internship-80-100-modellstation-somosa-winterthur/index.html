Role overview

SOMOSA offers an inpatient 6- to 12-month intensive care program for 14- to 18-year-old female and male adolescents in severe adolescent crisis.

The aim of the treatment is to stabilize the clients in their personality to such an extent that they can survive on leaving in the existing supporting or replacing socialization systems.

The treatment team consists of socio-pedagogical, psychiatric, psychotherapeutic and work-agogical specialists who implement the somosa-specific, therapeutic-pedagogical concept.
