Role overview

Responsibilities

  • Your area of responsibility includes the execution of client orders across all different asset classes, such as Equities/ETFs, Exchange-traded derivatives, OTC derivatives & Structured Products, as well as Fixed Income and Digital Assets
  • Provide execution consulting and support to Relationship Managers and Portfolio Management teams within Private Banking
  • Direct interaction with Direct Access & UHNW clients to facilitate the technical execution of orders
  • Play an integral role in driving continuous improvement of the client journey across all execution aspects in a multi-asset class approach
  • US hours shifts (02pm- 10pm CET), alternating with another person
  • Monitoring of orders from a best execution perspective and compliance with internal policies
  • Clarification of discrepancies in securities delivery and settlement
  • Dedicated support to our Ultra-High-Net-Worth clients for OTC equity derivatives, ensuring seamless implementation, optimal trade execution and support
  • Responsible for the accurate processing, monitoring, confirmation and settlement of derivatives transactions, ensuring strict adherence to service-level and compliance requirements
  • Demonstrates in-depth understanding of the end-to-end derivatives trade lifecycle, from execution to settlement

Collaboration & Governance

  • Collaborate across teams on process optimisation initiatives, adopting a global perspective through close coordination with all Julius Baer trading desks and key stakeholders worldwide
  • Work closely with risk, legal, compliance and operational partners to identify and meet applicable regulatory obligations
  • Regular contact with brokers, exchanges and regulatory authorities
  • Compliance with all regulatory international requirements including adherence to all Julius Baer internal policies

Requirements

  • Minimum of 3–4 years experience in executing client orders in OTC derivatives, exchange-traded derivatives, digital assets, and equities/ETFs is required
  • Deliver first-class coverage of client relationships, providing best-in-class service with a proactive, solution-oriented approach to meet the highest service standards
  • Demonstrate excellent problem-solving abilities, with a strong focus on delivering high-quality outcomes and driving continuous improvement in processes and systems
  • Possess the ability to collaborate effectively within teams and across functions, showing a strong commitment to excellence, integrity, and outstanding client service
  • Capable of quickly identifying and resolving issues by applying sound judgment to deliver efficient and effective solutions
  • Self-motivated, adaptable, and able to perform effectively under pressure
  • Demonstrates a positive mindset, passion for the role, and strong personal resilience
  • Highly motivated with a collaborative, team-player attitude
  • Able to work independently within a global team environment, taking initiative and contributing constructively with a constructive and positive attitude
  • Fluent in German and English is a must
