Role overview

In the Actuarial Services & Risk Modelling team, you support clients in analysing and evaluating complex actuarial and financial risks and making them transparent with data-driven models.

The team combines actuarial expertise with quantitative modeling, regulatory understanding and modern technologies to create sound decision-making bases.

Particularly exciting is the work on a wide range of projects in which topics such as risk management, evaluation, reservation, Solvency II, IFRS 17 and model validation play a central role.
