Role overview

As a non-profit organization, Spitex Zimmerberg AG is responsible for the care, advice, support and support of people of different age groups who are dependent on external help due to health restrictions. We enable the inhabitants of Adliswil, Thalwil, Kilchberg, Rüschlikon and Langnau am Albis to live a self-determined life in their usual environment. Spitex Zimmerberg AG has around 160 employees, spread over four locations and is one of the largest Spitex organizations in the Canton of Zurich. We are looking for you! To support our teams, as of July 1, 2026, we are looking for: Nurse HF/FH for the Competence Center Psychiatry 60-80 % Your tasks

  • You bring your expertise specifically in outpatient psychiatric care
  • As a case-leading care specialist, you are responsible for the entire care process of your customers and carry out needs assessments according to interRAI-CMH.
  • In close cooperation with relatives, doctors, clinics and other specialist centres, you provide holistic care
  • In the team, you represent your colleagues internally and externally and are actively involved in committees of the Competence Center Psychiatry
  • Within our self-organized team, you take on responsibility and actively participate in the further development of the competence center psychiatry

Requirement profile

  • Diploma as a nurse HF/FH with a focus on psychiatry
  • Several years of professional experience in inpatient psychiatry (ideally with ZSR number)
  • Enjoying a varied working environment as well as an independent, self-responsible working method
  • Responsibility for the entire care process of your customers
  • Diploma as a nurse HF/FH with a focus on psychiatry

Key requirements
