Project management «WohnFit – contact point for housing» 80% The “WohnFit” project offers support for people affected by poverty in the search for housing.

The aim of the offer is to contribute to equal opportunities on the Zurich housing market.

In the low-threshold contact points, volunteers develop search strategies with the target group, develop application documents and ensure access to digital devices and skills.

Specialists advise in precarious housing situations.

In the “WohnFit” project, practical knowledge is used specifically to achieve improvements at the structural level for people affected by poverty in the housing sector.
