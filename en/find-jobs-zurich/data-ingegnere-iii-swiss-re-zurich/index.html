Application process

  • Working closely with risk management experts, business stakeholders, data scientists, and technology teams, you will develop robust data pipelines, operational applications, and AI-powered solutions that transform complex business requirements into scalable, secure, and production-ready capabilities.

  • Ensure data governance, lineage, security, access controls, and compliance standards across data and AI assets.
  • Monitor, troubleshoot, and optimize data pipelines, AI workflows, and performance to improve scalability, reliability, and cost efficiency.
  • Document data assets, processes, and architectures, while contributing to engineering practices, and continuous improvement initiatives.
