Role overview

The Analytics, Reporting, Data & Platfoms division contributes to increasing the value of the Migros Group through the targeted use of data.

We use and build a modern data architecture on a state-of-the-art, cloud-based tech stack.

As a motivated data engineer, you will help us model and deploy various data sources on the Migros Analytics Data Platform.

  • The Analytics, Reporting, Data & Platfoms division contributes to increasing the value of the Migros Group through the targeted use of data.
  • We use and build a modern data architecture on a state-of-the-art, cloud-based tech stack.
  • You create automated data pipelines (dbt, SQL, Airflow/CloudComposer), as well as continuous integration and deployment workflows for ETL processes using CI/CD
  • You design and realize data models on the basis of DataVault 2.0, which represent the structure and relationships of the data from the areas of procurement and logistics
  • Relevant professional experience: in the area of data engineering (SQL, dbt, BigQuery, HF): Completed studies (Uni/ETH, physics, business informatics) or a comparable training
  • You have proven knowledge of common data warehouse principles and technologies (SQL, dbt, BigQuery, Terraform)
