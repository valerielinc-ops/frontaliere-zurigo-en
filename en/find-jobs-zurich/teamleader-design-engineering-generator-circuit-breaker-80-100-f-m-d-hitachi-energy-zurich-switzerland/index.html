Role overview

The opportunity Join our Generator Circuit Breaker (GCB) team and contribute to products that shape the reliability and performance of power generation worldwide.

In this role, you will lead and support a motivated team of CAD Designers while collaborating closely with experienced engineers, project leads suppliers, and the in Oerlikon located factory across the entire product lifecycle—from early design concepts to continuous improvement initiatives.

You will have the chance to make a real impact by driving high quality design practices, fostering unified working methods, and championing process and documentation excellence.
