Role overview

Canton of Zurich Financial Directorate Office for Informatics Application Engineer SAP HCM 80 - 100% The Office for Informatics (AFI) is the IT competence centre of the Canton of Zurich.

It provides comprehensive services in the area of central IT infrastructure and canton-wide applications for the directorates, authorities, legal administration and independent institutions.

Commissioned with the implementation of the Strategy Information and Communication Technology (ICT) supports and supports the Office for Informatics the Canton of Zurich into the digital world.

Together, we ensure that central HR processes function reliably and at the same time shape the future of the cantonal HR system landscape.

Whether it's operation, further development or digitalization, you can contribute your technical know-how and actively shape it.

Your tasks

  • Operation, maintenance and further development of SAP HCM solutions
  • Analysis and implementation of technical requirements in technical solutions
  • Cooperation and management of Changes, projects and subprojects in the SAP HCM environment
  • Support for the further development of the HR system landscape within the framework of the project AURORA (replacement of the existing SAP solution)
  • Advice of departments and customers in the personnel environment
  • Coordination of internal specialist offices and external partners
  • Ensure stable application operation and continuous optimization of the systems Your profile:
  • Higher education in computer science, business information technology or comparable qualifications
  • Experience in application management or in the management of complex business applications
  • Knowledge in the SAP HCM environment
  • Experience with ABAP/ABAP OO, Web Dynpro ABAP or SAP UI5 of advantage
  • Enjoying cooperation with different stakeholders
  • Self-employed, structured and solution-oriented approach Our offer:
  • Responsible task with a high impact for the entire canton of Zurich
  • Exciting environment between computer science, HR and digitalization
  • Working on a business the largest HR system landscapes in Switzerland
  • Modern forms of work and attractive employment conditions
  • Collegial team with lots of expertise and short decision-making methods
  • Mutual support, open and honest communication and appreciation in everyday life
  • Various opportunities for further education and development How to go on?

If we have awakened your enthusiasm, then apply to us.
