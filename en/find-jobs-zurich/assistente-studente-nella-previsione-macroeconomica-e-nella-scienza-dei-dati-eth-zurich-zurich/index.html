Student Assistant in Macroeconomic Forecasting and Data Science

20%-30%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken

The KOF Swiss Economic Institute at ETH Zurich is the leading institute for applied research in economics in Switzerland.

  • Student Assistant in Macroeconomic Forecasting and Data Science
  • 20%-30%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken

Company and context

  • Collecting macroeconomic data from public statistical offices, central banks, international organizations, and data providers such as Macrobond
  • Research on the availability of real-time data vintages, release calendars, publication lags, variable definitions, and data revisions
  • Cleaning, harmonizing, transforming, and documenting macroeconomic time series across countries, sectors, regions, and frequencies
  • Supporting the creation of a reproducible macroeconomic reanalysis dataset, including metadata, transformation codes, and versioning routines
  • Building and maintaining data acquisition routines, including APIs, web scraping, and automated downloads where feasible
  • Conducting descriptive analysis and consistency checks of macroeconomic indicators
  • Supporting the preparation of datasets for forecasting models, including dynamic factor models, BVARs, and machine-learning-based approaches. Profile
