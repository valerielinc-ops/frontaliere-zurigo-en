Role overview

Head of Alarm Organization (m/f/d) 100%, Zurich, permanent print Print The Facility Services department ensures the technical and infrastructural building management of all buildings and rooms used by ETH Zurich.

The affiliated alarm organization is divided into the alarm center (AZ) and security service (SiDi) sub-areas and works in 3 shifts (24/7/365).

The alarm center is the contact point for all incidents relating to personal, building and system protection.

The security service is responsible for checking the safety equipment in the buildings and the initial intervention in the event of an incident.
