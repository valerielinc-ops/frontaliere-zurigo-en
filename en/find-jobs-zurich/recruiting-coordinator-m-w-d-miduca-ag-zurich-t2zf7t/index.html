Role overview

You are an organizational talent, burn for the topic of recruiting, work in a structured way and also keep an overview in a dynamic environment? Do you want to make an important contribution to a professional and positive candidate experience and actively support our Talent Acquisition Partner? Then we should definitely get to know each other!

Mansioni

  • Administrative support in the entire recruitment process as well as deputies in the event of holiday absences
  • Management and maintenance of the applicant management system including screening of incoming applications
  • Preparation and tendering of job advertisements on various channels
  • Coordination and organisation of interviews
  • Participation in HR projects and implementation of virtual initial interviews with corresponding experience

Requirements

  • First professional experience: in recruiting, HR or in an administrative coordination function in HR
  • Completed vocational apprenticeship (EFZ): in the commercial sector, ideally with further training in human resources
  • French (good knowledge): further knowledge of foreign languages (English/Italian)
  • Good knowledge of MS-Office products and digital tools
  • Independent, structured and reliable working method
  • Strong organisational and communication skills as well as a high level of service and detail orientation

Cosa of Friamo

  • Central location: Central locations with excellent public transport connections
  • Modern infrastructure: modern, bright office space with ergonomic workstations
  • Occupational provision: Very attractive pension fund with above-average employer contribution
  • Sports and fitness offers: Cost sharing in subscriptions to various leisure and fitness facilities of the Migros Group
  • Self-responsibility & freedom: Varied tasks with a high level of self-responsibility
  • Leisure and cultural offers: Wide range of discounts (car rental, hotels, events, etc.)
