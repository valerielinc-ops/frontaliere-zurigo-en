Role overview

As a full-time housekeeper or full-time housekeeper at the Migros pension fund, you have a responsible task in which you can prove your strengths such as self-employment, organizational talent and flexibility on a daily basis. You look after our properties in miles, Herrliberg, Küsnacht and Erlenbach.

Mansioni

  • First contact person for our tenants
  • Assistance in the re-letting of rental properties (show apartments, etc.) as well as apartment acceptances and transfers (electronic with iPad)
  • Performing minor repairs and placing, coordinating and monitoring orders to craftsmen
  • Support of technical installations (heating/ventilation, etc.)
  • Care for the environment (discount care, lawn mowing, hedge cutting, etc.)
  • Cleaning of various external surfaces and general rooms (storage hall, heating, etc.)
  • Check and target invoices for correctness and completeness (electronic)
  • Readiness to perform special operations in exceptional cases (winter service)

Requirements

  • At least 5 years: in house maintenance
  • Completed apprenticeship (EFZ): maintenance or technical/craft profession
  • Driving licence Cat. B
  • Independent and reliable working method
  • Experience as a housekeeper/residential property
  • Good manners, service-oriented and communicative personality with pleasure in contact with tenants and craftsmen
  • Experience in dealing with iPad, computer science user knowledge
  • Eidg. Professional ID as a housekeeper/in advantage
  • German (very good knowledge)
  • English (advantageous)

Cosa of Friamo

  • Flexible working hours: Liberal working time regulations with a lot of flexibility for employees
  • Social benefits: Excellent pension fund and generous sickness allowance and accident insurance
  • Holidays: Large Trains Holiday Regulation
  • Self-Responsibility & Freedom: Great design opportunity in the workplace
  • Du-culture: Uncomplicated and open approach
