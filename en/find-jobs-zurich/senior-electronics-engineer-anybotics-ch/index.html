Role overview

ANYbotics is a fast-growing tech company dedicated to shaping the future of mobile robotics across multiple industries.

Join our highly talented and motivated team of more than 200 people and work on cutting-edge robot technology. The Opportunity

We are looking for an experienced and driven Senior Electronics Engineer to join our Hardware Engineering team.

Application process

  • Degree in Electrical/Electronics Engineering or a related field with at least 7 years of hands-on experience developing electronics boards for industrial, aerospace, or military applications
  • Expert-level proficiency in industry-standard ECAD software (e.g., KiCad, Altium) and simulation tools like SPICE
  • Proven track record of layouting your own complex designs with strict creepage, clearance, and isolation constraints for safety-critical applications
  • Extensive experience designing for harsh environments, including a strong background in meeting EMI/EMC compliance and functional safety standards
  • Deep expertise in debugging, verification, and defining testing specifications for manufacturing production
  • Strong proficiency in at least two of the following areas: Battery Management Systems (BMS), power electronics/motor drives, high-speed interfaces (USB, EtherCAT, WiFi), or MCU-based embedded systems
  • Highly accountable mindset with the ability to make firm technical decisions and own the outcomes in a dynamic environment Bonus Points
  • Experience with Explosion-proof (Ex-protection) electronics design or hazardous-area applications
