Description

Are you a strategic thinker with strong analytical capabilities and a passion for driving complex M&A transactions? Do you combine rigorous financial analysis with the ability to deliver clear insights to senior stakeholders?

We’re looking for an Associate Director to

  • support and selectively lead strategic M&A initiatives across UBS Group
  • identify, evaluate and execute M&A transactions and related strategic projects end-to-end
  • conduct detailed market research, financial analysis and valuation work to assess potential targets and strategic options
  • develop and maintain complex financial models to support decision-making
  • synthesize quantitative and qualitative analyses into clear, concise recommendations for senior management
  • prepare high-quality presentations and materials for internal and external stakeholders
  • collaborate closely with business divisions, group functions and external advisors
