Role overview

Tasks

  • Staff management of non-medical staff in the assigned practices in close coordination with the medical site manager
  • Project management and control in the field of practices

  • Quality management officer of the entire practice network (sicing and further development of a uniform quality standard)

  • Strong social skills and a high sense of responsibility

  • Where possible, very good knowledge of Axenita
