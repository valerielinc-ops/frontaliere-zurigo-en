Role overview

Our rehabilitation clinic Limmattal offers specialized musculoskeletal and neurological rehabilitation including surveillance rehabilitation and accident rehabilitation at a high medical level.

Through targeted diagnostics and individually tailored therapy programs, our interdisciplinary team ensures that our patients are optimally looked after on their way back to better health and quality of life.

Due to the close cooperation with the Hospital Limmattal and the direct connection to the medical emergency unit, prompt acute somatic treatment is always ensured even in the event of emergency complications.
