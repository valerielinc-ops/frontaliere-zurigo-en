Key requirements

  • 3+ years of experience as a senior architect, 5+ years of experience as a lead or senior software engineer
  • Responsible for solution architectures: from design and implementation to operation
  • Ability to identify options for solution architectures and make trade-off architecture decisions
  • Experience with SAP solutions, especially S4/HANA, BW/HANA, BTP
  • Good understanding of Financial Accounting, Management Accounting
  • Knowledge of IFRS and Swiss GAAP in financial service industries
  • Analysing complex business requirements and translating them into technical solutions
  • Ability to abstract complex topics and focus on the key issues that need architecture decisions
  • Collaboratively develop and coordinate solutions with business and technology stakeholders and architecture colleagues
  • Proactive mindset, able to work under pressure and meet deadlines
  • Proven experience in solution design and knowledge of current architecture styles, such as event-driven, microservices, modular monolith
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills

Application process

  • You design and are responsible for the solution architecture for applications in delivery
  • You ensure that your solution architectures in delivery adhere to architecture standards and are aligned with the target architectures of the domain
  • You raise and manage architecture debt when there is a misalignment between transition and the target architectures
  • You initiate architecture decisions, document them as Architecture Decision Records (ADR) and have them approved by the relevant architecture boards
  • You advise and coach software engineers on the implementation of solution architectures and ensure technical feasibility
  • In addition to architecture design, you also support later phases of the software lifecycle, such as testing, release and operations
