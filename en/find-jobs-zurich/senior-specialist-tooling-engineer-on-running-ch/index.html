Role overview

This role will act as the leader and main point of contact when it comes to the bottom unit in the footwear development process.

As a Footwear Tooling engineer, you will work in close collaboration with designers, developers and out sourcing hub in Asia to provide a smooth and effective transition from the concept creation, to prototyping and through to production.

You will also be working closely with our Innovation and Sport engineering teams to help validate and bring high performance products and technologies to market.
