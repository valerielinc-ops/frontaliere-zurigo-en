Role overview

Are you at the start of your change management journey and eager to grow into a role where you can make a real impact?

Change doesn't manage itself — and that's exactly where you come in.

As a Change Enablement Specialist, you'll be supporting some of our most strategically important initiatives, helping to ensure that the people impacted by change are informed, prepared, and set up for success.

Main responsibilities

  • Key responsibilities
  • Support change activities across key projects and business initiatives.
  • Coordinate activities across teams and stakeholders to ensure everyone stays informed and aligned.
  • Work with project teams, business stakeholders, and leaders to support successful change adoption.
  • Help identify impacted groups, assess their needs, and understand how changes may affect them.
  • Support the creation and delivery of change communications and engagement plans.
  • Track change readiness and adoption by gathering feedback, running surveys, and monitoring key metrics.
  • Identify potential adoption risks and help address them early.
  • Coordinate go-live activities and help prepare employees for upcoming changes.
  • Support reporting and maintain visibility of change activities, timelines, and readiness status.
