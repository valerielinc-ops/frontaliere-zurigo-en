Main responsibilities

  • As head of the Energy Technology section, you are responsible for the further development of building regulations and the processing of political business in the field of energy technology.

  • For this demanding task, you will bring with you a degree in engineering or natural scientist and a multi-year experience in energy engineering issues in the building sector.
