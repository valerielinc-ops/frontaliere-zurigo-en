Canton of Zurich Directorate of Justice and Interior Prison Imprisonment and Reintegration Prison Pöschwies Springerin / Springer, temporary 80

100% Prison Imprisonment and Reintegration (JuWe) is responsible as part of the Directorate of Justice and Interior of the Canton of Zurich for the execution of penalties and measures as well as the execution of the various types of detention.

For our 1,400 employees from around 40 professions, the successful social reintegration of people who have committed crimes and the prevention of recidivism are of particular importance.
