Application process

  • Software Solutions, you will shape the future of Sonova's global fitting software ecosystem by leading the end-to-end delivery, integration and evolution of scalable, brand-agnostic fitting software applications used across multiple brands and regions.
  • You will ensure our solutions deliver meaningful audiological value while meeting the highest standards of quality, scalability and regulatory compliance.
  • Working within our globally integrated R&D organization, you'll collaborate across engineering, product and business functions to drive predictable delivery of fitting software applications, enable technical excellence and influence the long-term evolution of Sonova's fitting software portfolio.
  • In this highly visible leadership role, you'll help shape software solutions that empower hearing care professionals and improve the lives of millions of people with hearing loss worldwide. More about the role
  • Lead the end-to-end delivery of fitting software applications, from feature development through integration, stabilization and release readiness.
  • Drive the integration, evolution and lifecycle management of fitting software applications and variants, ensuring scalable, robust and brand-agnostic solutions across global markets.
  • Translate product priorities into executable delivery plans while managing dependencies, timelines, delivery risks and cross-functional trade-offs across multiple release trains.
  • Ensure successful software integration, verification readiness, stabilization activities and release governance in close collaboration with System Integration, Platform & Component
