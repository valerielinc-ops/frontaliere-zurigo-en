Role overview

In this versatile role, you will contribute significantly to the optimization of daily work in clinics by translating medical requirements into technical solutions and further developing the clinic information system.

Your commitment ensures that medical professionals are sustainably relieved and modern, reliable IT solutions are successfully anchored in hospital operations.

The University Hospital Zurich is one of the largest hospitals in Switzerland.

  • In this versatile role, you will contribute significantly to the optimization of daily work in clinics by translating medical requirements into technical solutions and further developing the clinic information system.
  • Your commitment ensures that medical professionals are sustainably relieved and modern, reliable IT solutions are successfully anchored in hospital operations.
  • As a member of the agile team KIS, together with the product owner and the team coach, you implement requirements from the clinics into technical specifications.
