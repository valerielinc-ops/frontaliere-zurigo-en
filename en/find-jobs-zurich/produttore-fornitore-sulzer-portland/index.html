Main responsibilities

  • Essential Duties and Responsibilities
  • Perform high-quality MIG and TIG welding on stainless steel components and assemblies in accordance with engineering drawings, weld procedures, and work instructions.
  • Read, interpret, and work from blueprints, fabrication drawings, specifications, and welding process requirements.
  • Lay out, measure, mark, cut, fit, and form materials to meet dimensional and quality specifications.
  • Fabricate, assemble, and repair metal components and equipment using appropriate welding and fabrication techniques.
  • Troubleshoot complex fabrication and assembly issues, utilizing technical expertise and sound judgment to develop effective solutions.
  • Perform self-inspections throughout the fabrication process to ensure parts and assemblies conform to print requirements, tolerances, and quality standards.
  • Set up and adjust welding equipment, tooling, and welding parameters according to work specifications.
  • Perform routine inspections, calibration verification, cleaning, and basic maintenance of welding equipment, machines, torches, cables, and related tools.
  • Identify equipment defects and coordinate or perform minor repairs as appropriate to maintain safe and efficient operation.
