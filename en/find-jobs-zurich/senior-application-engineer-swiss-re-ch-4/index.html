Senior Application Engineer Hyderabad, India | Hybrid Are you a seasoned engineer who thrives at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and complex business challenges? About the Role As a Senior Application Engineer, you will be at the heart of designing and delivering robust, enterprise-grade applications that power Swiss Re's Life & Health Reinsurance business. You'll collaborate with Product Owners, Business Analysts, and Architects across global teams to craft solutions that are not only technically sound but strategically aligned with business goals. This is a role where your engineering depth, your curiosity, and your ability to lead will all be put to work — every single day. Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement end-to-end application and product lifecycle solutions, ensuring scalability, reliability, and performance across enterprise systems
  • Collaborate closely with Product Owners, Business Analysts, and Architects to translate business requirements into pragmatic, future-proof technical designs
  • Assess and manage technical risk, prioritize non-functional requirements, and champion engineering best practices across the team
  • Lead and mentor junior engineers, fostering a culture of continuous learning, knowledge sharing, and technical excellence
  • Drive cloud-native development on platforms such as Azure, contributing to the evolution of modern, microservices-based architectures
  • Contribute to AI-driven development practices, leveraging frontier AI models to enhance the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and product delivery
  • Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines, ensuring smooth, automated delivery of high-quality software using tools like Maven and Git
  • Engage across global, cross-functional teams — both internal and external — to align on solutions, share knowledge, and deliver outcomes collaboratively
