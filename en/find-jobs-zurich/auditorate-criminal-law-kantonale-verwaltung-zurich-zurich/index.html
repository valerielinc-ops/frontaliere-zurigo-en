Canton of Zurich Directorate of Justice and Home Affairs Statutory Office District of Zurich Auditorate ( criminal law) 100% The Zurich Statutory Office is responsible for numerous tasks in the district of Zurich as a decentralized cantonal administrative unit. The focus is on the prosecution of violations. We are looking for a legal trainee or a legal intern (100%) for the cooperation in our criminal law department as of 1 September 2026.
