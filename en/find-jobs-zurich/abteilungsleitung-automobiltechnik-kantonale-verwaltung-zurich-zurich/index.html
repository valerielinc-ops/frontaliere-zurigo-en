Canton of Zurich Education Directorate Technical Vocational School Zurich TBZ Head of the Automotive Engineering Department 100% The Automotive Engineering Department of the Technical Vocational School Zurich TBZ is the competence center for the initial and continuing training of the automotive and carrosserie professions.
