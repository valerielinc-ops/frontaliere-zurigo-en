Postdoctoral Position in Ultrafast Laser Spectroscopy of Quantum Materials

100%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken

The FERROIC Lab (Laboratory for Functional Ferroic Materials) at ETH Zurich applies advanced ultrafast laser spectroscopy from the Terahertz to the ultraviolet spectral range to discover, control and understand novel quantum phenomena in condensed-matter systems.
