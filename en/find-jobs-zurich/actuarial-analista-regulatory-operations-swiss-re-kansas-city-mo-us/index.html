Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities
  • Lead and manage rate and rating rule filings, rating factor adjustments, and rate implementation processes across multiple states, systems and lines of business
  • Mentor and support the globally located Actuarial Services Unit (ASU) staff in Bangalore, India, providing guidance, solutions, and oversight on all ASU projects and deliverables
  • Oversee and drive database creation and the ongoing update of generic filing templates to ensure accuracy and efficiency
  • Negotiate with Departments of Insurance (DOIs) and coordinate with pricing actuaries to develop and deliver effective rate objection responses
  • Lead rate filing planning discussions and proactively communicate updates to key stakeholders including IT, Underwriting, and Products teams
  • Implement and manage the compliance rate audit process for all business units in the CorSo North America region, including new products and platforms
  • Analyze, interpret, and communicate annual by-state, by-line-of-business rate indication analyses, rate implementations, and impact calculations
  • Drive special projects in collaboration with the Pricing Actuarial Department, providing actuarial support around rate adequacy factors such as LCMs and trends
  • Contribute to strategic initiatives and new product development within Corporate Solutions About the Team
