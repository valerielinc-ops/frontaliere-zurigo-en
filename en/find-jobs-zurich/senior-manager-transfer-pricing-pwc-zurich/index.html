Description

Within PwC's Tax & Legal Workforce, the Transfer Pricing team supports multinational clients in navigating complex global tax environments. The team works across borders, combining economic, legal and financial expertise to design and implement sustainable and compliant business models. Collaboration, knowledge sharing and continuous development are at the core of how the team operates, enabling professionals to deliver high-quality solutions while growing their expertise in an international and dynamic environment.

  • Manage, direct and coordinate multi-country transfer pricing projects for multinational groups. Develop concepts for the design of new or the optimization of existing business models, as well as the subsequent implementation of such models on a global basis.
  • Be involved in scoping, planning and delivering solutions for some of the world's leading companies across a wide range of industries.
