Role overview

Eraneos is an international strategy, transformation and technology consulting group, dedicated to empowering organizations to thrive in an ever-changing digital age. By bringing together top-tier experts from business and tech, we help clients to continuously raise the bar in successful transformations, from strategy to execution. Whether we’re designing future-ready organizations, unlocking the potential of data and AI, or securing businesses with cutting-edge cybersecurity, we deliver results pragmatically. Our team of around 1,200 dedicated professionals is based in offices across Switzerland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Austria, the UK, Singapore, and the USA, and seamlessly blends global perspectives with strong local roots. Around 450 employees work at our Swiss locations in Zurich, Bern and Lausanne.

  • Serve as a subject-matter expert for Veeva Vault CRM, leading implementations and configuration activities in Life Sciences projects.
  • Advise and support clients on best practices, compliance, and regulatory requirements when designing Veeva Vault CRM solutions.
  • Lead workshops, gather and analyse business requirements, and translate them into scalable Vault CRM architectures.
  • Oversee system integration and migration activities, ensuring data integrity, quality, and adherence to project timelines.
  • Collaborate closely with interdisciplinary teams to ensure successful delivery and adoption of Veeva Vault CRM solutions.
  • You have hands-on experience with Veeva Vault CRM and ideally hold relevant Veeva certifications.
  • You are passionate about driving digital transformation in the pharmaceutical or broader life sciences industry.
  • You enjoy working in a dynamic, project-based environment and feel confident collaborating with diverse stakeholders.
  • You communicate effectively in both German and English (spoken and written) and can explain complex topics clearly and concisely.
