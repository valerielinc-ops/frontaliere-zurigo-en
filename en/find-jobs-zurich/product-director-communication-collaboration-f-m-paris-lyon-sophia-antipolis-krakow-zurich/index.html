Company and context

  • We are building the first AI Employee Hub, helping organizations communicate, collaborate, and automate work so teams can focus on what matters most.
  • We are looking for an experienced Product Director to lead one of our product tribes, Communication & Collaboration (C&C), which is the core of the LumApps platform, focused on creating and distributing engaging content, knowledge management, and real-time collaboration features for thousands of customers and millions of users.
  • This role is critical in shaping the vision and strategy for the tribe, driving roadmap execution, and ensuring delivery of customer and business value.
  • You will be responsible for translating strategy into action, enabling your teams to excel, and ensuring we meet adoption, usage, and satisfaction goals.
  • As Product Director, you’ll be both a strategist and an operator: defining and maintaining a clear roadmap, mentoring product managers, and representing the voice of our customers and buyer personas inside and outside the company.
  • You will be the Product Lead of a dynamic tribe composed of 4 teams (CMS, Social, Play, Frontline Comms), directly managing 3–4 Product Managers.
  • The teams are distributed across our main R&D hubs, Paris, Lyon, Sophia Antipolis, Zurich, and Krakow, making this a truly international and collaborative environment.
  • Working closely with engineering, design, and other cross-functional partners, you will play a key role in driving alignment, empowering teams, and ensuring consistent delivery across locations. Missions*
  • Set the tribe strategy & portfolio: Define and maintain a strategic, data-informed tribe roadmap aligned to company objectives, customer needs, and market trends; make the “why” explicit and measurable.
  • Drive execution across multiple teams: Ensure alignment of team-level roadmaps, sequence bets, manage interdependencies (within and across tribes), and deliver against commitments.
