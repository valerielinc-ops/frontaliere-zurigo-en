Role overview

In the clinic for radiology and nuclear medicine, more than 105,000 examinations and interventions are carried out annually.

The Clinic of Radiology and Nuclear Medicine ensures the highest level of expertise and quality of care in all its areas: X-ray and fluoroscopy diagnostics, breast imaging, ultrasound, nuclear medicine, magnetic resonance imaging, computed tomography, pediatric radiology, neuroradiology and interventional radiology.

In order to strengthen our team and expand our equipment park, we are looking for a dedicated and qualified medical-technical radiology specialist HF (MTR HF) for computed tomography.
