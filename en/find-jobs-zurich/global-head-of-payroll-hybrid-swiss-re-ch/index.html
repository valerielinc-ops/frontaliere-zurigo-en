Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities
  • Lead and develop a high-performing global payroll team, setting clear expectations, building capability through coaching and feedback in order to effectively deliver end-to-end payroll operations across multiple countries and regions — including complex scenarios such as international assignments, expatriate payroll, and cross-border payments
  • Drive payroll transformation and innovation, including process standardisation, system enhancements and integrations, and automation/AI initiatives to continuously improve efficiency, accuracy, and the overall employee payroll experience
  • Own the payroll governance, controls and compliance framework, ensuring audit readiness, data security, and adherence to regulatory requirements across all jurisdictions, in close partnership with Finance, Risk, and Audit
  • Manage vendor relationships and performance, leading strategy, procurement, and contract negotiations for payroll outsourcing services, and overseeing contractual outcomes with the global payroll provider, including assurance reviews (SOC 1/SOC 2) and IT security alignment
  • Oversee financial management and year-end activities, including budget forecasting, cost transparency, tax reporting, annual statements, and statutory filings across all jurisdictions
  • Ensure robust payroll systems and data integrity, working closely with HR and Finance to accurately reflect payroll requirements in systems architecture, including upstream/downstream interfaces and data quality controls
  • Leverage analytics and market insights to provide reporting on payroll costs, trends, service performance, and risks, while staying current on emerging best practices, regulatory developments, and technology innovations in global payroll About the Team
  • The Global Payroll team at Swiss Re plays a key role in delivering accurate, timely, and compliant payroll services for employees around the world.
  • Operating in a highly collaborative and international environment, the team partners closely with HR, Finance, and external providers to manage end-to-end payroll operations, statutory reporting, and governance across multiple countries.
