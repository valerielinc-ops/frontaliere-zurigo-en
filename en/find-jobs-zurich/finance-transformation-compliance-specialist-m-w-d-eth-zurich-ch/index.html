In the area of Vice President Finance and Controlling (VPFC), the newly created Financial Management & Compliance department plays a central role in the management and monitoring of regulatory standards.

Company and context

  • Initially, the focus will be on the introduction of an organization-wide expense and travel tool – then you will play a central role in the further development of ETH Zurich’s financial compliance.
  • In the first 12 to 18 months, you will assume overall technical responsibility for the introduction of an organization-wide expense and travel tool.
  • You will analyse existing processes, develop standardised and sustainable target processes, define control and approval mechanisms and control cooperation with internal stakeholders from finance, IT, HR and departments.
  • You contribute your expertise in the selection and implementation of the system solution, coordinate the implementation and support rollout, training and documentation.
  • After the successful introduction of the tool, your focus increasingly shifts to financial compliance.
  • They actively develop compliance issues in the financial sector and contribute significantly to the operation and expansion of financial compliance.
  • They create and update financial guidelines and regulations, develop training and awareness-raising measures and advise organizational units on financial compliance-relevant issues.
  • In addition, you will conduct risk analysis and internal audits, ensuring that regulatory requirements are translated into pragmatic and sustainable solutions.
  • In this role, you work independently, networked and with high impact across organizational boundaries.
