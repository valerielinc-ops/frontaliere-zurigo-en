Main responsibilities

  • Key Responsibilities
  • Own the client relationship end-to-end: Serve as the central point of contact for your L&H clients in Italy, ensuring seamless communication and consistently high-quality service delivery
  • Develop and execute growth strategies: Identify and implement opportunities for existing and prospective clients, leveraging actuarial expertise in pricing, product development, and portfolio steering
  • Drive pricing and profitability analysis: Work cross-functionally and directly with clients to price and analyse the profitability of life reinsurance treaties, supporting the development and pricing of new life insurance products
  • Monitor portfolio performance: Analyse emerging claims experience and steer portfolio outcomes through data-driven insights and proactive management
  • Build trusted, long-term client relationships: Position Swiss Re as the leading and preferred reinsurance partner in the Italian market through strategic engagement and professional credibility
  • Manage client relationships holistically: Oversee the financial performance of in-force portfolios and evaluate and structure new business opportunities
  • Expand market presence: Build and maintain a strong professional network within the Italian insurance market to identify and execute opportunities across Swiss Re's strategic pillars — Core, Solutions, and Transactions
  • Represent Swiss Re externally: Act as a credible technical ambassador at client meetings, industry events, conferences, and professional forums About the Team
  • You will join a dynamic and highly motivated team committed to delivering outstanding results and shaping the future of Swiss Re's business.
