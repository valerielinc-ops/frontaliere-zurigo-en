Role overview

Canton of Zurich Education Directorate Office for Youth and Career Counselling Psychology Practitioner/in Education Counselling 60% The Office for Youth and Vocational Counselling of the Education Directorate provides, appoints, controls and coordinates offers of child and youth welfare and vocational counselling.

As one of a total of 15 child and youth welfare centres in the Canton of Zurich, kjz Winterthur guarantees a uniform, regionally and municipally solidly supported provision of services in the areas of youth and family counselling, parental counselling, maternal and paternal counselling and child protection.

The kjz Winterthur offers students of psychology (bachelor or master) at a Swiss university or university a six-month internship from 1 February 2027.
