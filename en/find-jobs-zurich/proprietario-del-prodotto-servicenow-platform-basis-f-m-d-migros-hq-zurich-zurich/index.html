Actively shape Migros’ ServiceNow platform as a strategic core platform: you connect demand management, governance, architecture and operations.

Your goal: clear priorities, a stable, long-term upgrade-capable platform and maximum business value.

Main responsibilities

  • You work closely with the architect, quality manager, manager of operations and demand team and actively promote the technical and professional development of the platform

Company and context

  • You see ServiceNow as a strategic platform and can transform demand, architecture, operations, technical development and organizational governance into an effective operating model.
  • You are responsible for the stable operation of the platform, ensuring transparency in terms of risks, dependencies and opportunities as well as improvements
  • You implement binding group-wide standards as well as reusable platform modules and basic services in all areas of Migros using the core modules of the SN platform
  • You are the central contact person for the division management and take responsibility for backlog, prioritization and communication of platform-base topics
  • As a Design Authority, you are responsible for architectural standards for scaled developments and integrations, ensuring cross-team implementation, maintainability and long-term upgrade capability. Requirements
  • You have several years of practical ServiceNow experience in an agile environment as a platform or product owner, or in an architecture-oriented leadership role: ideally in a hybrid IT environment with multiple stakeholders and a high degree of integration with proven experience in managing teams and projects
  • Completed studies (uni/ETH, FH, HF): in computer science or business informatics, ideally with further training in Service Now (CTA, CPOA) and agility (SAFe, Scrum)
  • You have several years of practical experience with ServiceNow as well as a deep understanding of company-wide platform technologies, processes (ITIL) and their continuous improvement
  • Issues such as upgradeability, data model discipline, standardization, reusability, controlled extensibility and the avoidance of uncontrolled customization are known
  • You will provide a deep understanding of the SN platform architecture (instances, sub-prod, releases), integrations to environments, and operational processes (ServiceNow best practices) and their automation.
