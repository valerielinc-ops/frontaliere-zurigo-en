Application process

  • An exciting position awaits you: you do not simply check applications, you ensure that our leasing transactions are carried out safely, efficiently and customer-oriented.
  • Examination of lease applications for completeness, plausibility and quality
  • Conducting legally compliant credit and risk analyses (GWG, KKG)
  • Processing of contract changes such as rewrites and renewals
  • Telephone & written advice for dealers, partners and end customers
  • Support of the sales force in the implementation of our sales strategy You bring:
  • You have a completed commercial education.
  • Ideally, with experience in finance or mobility.
