Role overview

In short

As the Head of Merchandising Planning EMEA, you will be the strategic visionary leading our merchandising planning function across the region.

You will architect the planning strategy that powers our commercial success, ensuring our product vision translates seamlessly into data-driven, highly accurate, and scalable financial and inventory plans.

  • As the Head of Merchandising Planning EMEA, you will be the strategic visionary leading our merchandising planning function across the region.

Additional details

  • You are a key leadership partner, integrating planning insights into the broader Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy bridging the gap between global product intent and regional market reality, and aligning our multi-channel execution across Wholesale, Digital, and Retail.
  • Team Leadership & Upskilling: Directly manage, mentor, and inspire a high-performing team of Merchandising Planning Leads, fostering a culture of data-driven accountability and operational excellence.
  • Fluent in English (written and verbal), with additional languages considered a plus.
  • Your team will consist of planning leads and specialists focussed on both footwear and apparel.
