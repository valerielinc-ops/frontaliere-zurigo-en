Role overview

Sulzer is a leading engineering company with a proud heritage of innovation.

Join our global team to grow your expertise and develop innovative solutions that enable a prosperous and more sustainable society.

The Southern California Area Sales Manager (ASM) is the primary external representative of Sulzer’s Electro Mechanical Services (EMS) and is financially responsible for the Order Intake, Gross Profit Margin, and Operating Net Cash-Flow.

Main responsibilities

  • Meet or exceed the Order Intake budget for the assigned area/product line

  • Develop and implement sales strategies consistent with the goals of Sulzer
  • Plan, support and monitor sales activity within the area/product line
  • Develop and deliver technical and commercially superior proposals to the client
  • Support all business activities with the client thru closing and invoicing
  • Develop, maintain and report accurate sales forecasts
  • Review of RFPs, contracts, POs and terms for all business opportunities
  • Control all business expenses as if they were your own

Key requirements

  • Previous experience of proactively influencing or closing business with clients, co-workers and/or commercial peers.
  • In-depth knowledge of Motors, generators and learn Sulzer’s complete product line
  • Must be able to understand, interpret and discuss technical and commercial documents.
  • High personal standards of character.
