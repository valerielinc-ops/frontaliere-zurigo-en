Role overview

In the Clinic for Radiology and Nuclear Medicine, over 105,000 examinations and procedures are carried out every year.

The Clinic for Radiology and Nuclear Medicine guarantees the highest level of expertise and quality of care in all of its areas: X-ray and fluoroscopy diagnostics, breast imaging, ultrasound, nuclear medicine, magnetic resonance imaging, computer tomography, pediatric radiology, neuroradiology and interventional radiology.

To strengthen our team, we are looking for a committed and qualified medical-technical radiology specialist HF (MTR HF) for the field of interventional radiology, immediately or by appointment.

Main responsibilities

  • Assistance with diagnostic and therapeutic interventional-radiological procedures in compliance with radiation protection guidelines
  • Preparation, care and monitoring of patients before, during and after the interventions
  • Operation and quality control of modern imaging devices (angiography systems, CT)
  • Close interdisciplinary collaboration with doctors, nursing staff and other specialist departments
  • Participation in the further development of work processes and quality assurance Profile:
  • Completed training as an MTR HF or equivalent in Switzerland Recognized training
  • Ideally experience in interventional radiology or a high willingness to train
  • Responsible, independent and precise way of working
  • High social skills, ability to work in a team and resilience
  • Flexibility for on-call or on-call services Benefits: The KSW offers employees conditions that enable them to balance their professional goals and personal needs.
