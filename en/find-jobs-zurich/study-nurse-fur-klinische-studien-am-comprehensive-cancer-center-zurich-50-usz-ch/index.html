Role overview

At the Comprehensive Cancer Center Zurich (CCCZ), we develop and conduct more than 100 clinical trials every year to treat cancer.

In this way, we enable patients to access innovative therapeutic approaches and the latest state of science.

In this versatile role, you combine the direct care of study patients with the coordination and further development of our clinical trials program. >The position is organized as a job sharing model.

  • At the Comprehensive Cancer Center Zurich (CCCZ), we develop and conduct more than 100 clinical trials every year to treat cancer.
  • In this way, we enable patients to access innovative therapeutic approaches and the latest state of science.
  • Close cooperation with the study centres of the CCCZ and with interprofessional partners:
  • Supporting the CCCZ Director Clinical Trials in the coordination and development of the clinical trials program

Main responsibilities

  • Close cooperation with the study centres of the CCCZ and with interprofessional partners:
  • Supporting the CCCZ Director Clinical Trials in the coordination and development of the clinical trials program
  • Care of study patients: in the CCCZ-Day Clinic and in the future CCCZ Clinical Trial Unit
  • Administration of study drugs according to study protocol, with focus on safe and professional implementation
  • Organisation of regular exchange meetings of the CCCZ’s study centres and ensuring structured cooperation
  • Supervision of the CCCZ Clinical Trial Finder (hts://krebszentrum-zurich.ch/en/studien/ ) and further development of the clinical trials outreach programme
  • Participation in the introduction of a platform for recording study activity and strengthening the local network for clinical trials in Zurich and the surrounding area Requirements
  • Diplomatic nurse HF/FH with experience in the care of patients: inside in clinical studies and in the administration of tumor therapies;
  • Experience with Phase I/II studies is beneficial
  • Current, Switzerland-recognized GCP certification (modules 1 + 2) or willingness to complete it in a timely manner

Additional details

  • Participation in the introduction of a platform for recording study activity and strengthening the local network for clinical trials in Zurich and the surrounding area
  • Clear, appreciative communication and enjoyment of working in an interprofessional and international environment Very good German and English skills
  • Experience with MS Office, especially Excel and PowerPoint, ideally with experience in eCRFs such as secuTrial® or REDCap®
  • Regulated daily working hours, no shift operation.
