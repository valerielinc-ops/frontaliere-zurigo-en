Start August 2027 | 100% | Canton of Zurich | Students

Coding, bytes and clouds are your world? During our four-year computer science course, you will get to know our IT in its various fields. You acquire the necessary know-how to start directly after the teaching.

We offer you a future-oriented, exciting and varied vocational training – your ideal start in the world of computer science. The Zürcher Kantonalbank is one of the largest training companies in the canton of Zurich and supports more than 420 apprentices every year. Would you like to be part of our Young Talents? We look forward to your application!

  • Learning all important basic skills during basic training in our ZKB IT Academy
  • Participation in training and internal events to further develop your personal, social and methodological skills
  • Get to know and acquire the secrets of several programming languages
  • Develop programs that support various areas of our bank (such as Twint or eBanking)
  • Planning of orders from programming to testing up to handover to clients
  • Graduation from secondary school A with a grade average of 4,625 in major subjects (mathematics including geometry, German, English)*
  • Good Multicheck ICT – Evaluation Computer Scientist or Computer Scientist Application/Platform Development
  • You are enthusiastic about the computer science world and you like to solve complex tasks
  • Learning new things and moving things give you pleasure
  • You like to communicate and have fun working with others
  • Required application documents: letter of application/motivation, curriculum vitae, all four upper-level certificates, Multicheck ICT, location determination of the secondary level (interface test, Check S2 or similar)

*The grade averages given refer to the grades in level 1. For compartments in level 2, the grade must be correspondingly higher.
