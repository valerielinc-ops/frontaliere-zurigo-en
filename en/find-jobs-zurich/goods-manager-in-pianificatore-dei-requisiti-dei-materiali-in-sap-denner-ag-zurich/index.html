Main responsibilities

  • Process the procurement of the assigned goods group in the SAP S/4 HANA ERP system.
  • You provide an impeccable and forward-looking disposition incl.
  • Action orders at the suppliers safely.
  • You ensure optimal product availability in the distribution centers, monitor the daily reports and analyze the key figures.
  • You work on the continuous improvement and consistent further development of the disposition processes.
  • You maintain the master data in the scheduling area, which requires a regular exchange with purchasing and logistics.
  • You support strategic purchasing for listings and exclusions of items by means of inventory calculations and their reach. Requirements
  • 4-5 years of professional experience: Relevant experience in this or related field
  • Completed vocational apprenticeship (EFZ): professional training in purchasing or procurement logistics
  • User knowledge in SAP is mandatory, as is an experienced handling of MS-Office, especially Excel.
