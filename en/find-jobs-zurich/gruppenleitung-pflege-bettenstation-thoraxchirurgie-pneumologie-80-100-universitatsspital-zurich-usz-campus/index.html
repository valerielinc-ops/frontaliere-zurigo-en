Role overview

We offer you the opportunity to take on a leadership position in a dynamic and highly specialized environment of thoracic surgery and pulmonology.

On our university bedside ward, you look after patients with complex thoracic surgical and pneumological clinical pictures and actively shape the further development of an evidence-based nursing practice.

Participation, interdisciplinary cooperation and a constructive team culture are very important to us.

  • We offer you the opportunity to take on a leadership position in a dynamic and highly specialized environment of thoracic surgery and pulmonology.
  • On our university bedside ward, you look after patients with complex thoracic surgical and pneumological clinical pictures and actively shape the further development of an evidence-based nursing practice.
  • You care for patients with complex thoracic surgical and pulmonary diseases (e.g. after pulmonary resections, respiratory insufficiency or oncological therapies) and ensure evidence-based, safe care throughout the treatment process
  • As part of the day-to-day responsibility, you coordinate the processes on the bed station, prioritize resources and act as the central contact person for interdisciplinary professional groups along the patient path.
