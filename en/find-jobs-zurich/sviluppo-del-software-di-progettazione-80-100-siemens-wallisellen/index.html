Main responsibilities

  • Your duties and responsibilities
  • You plan, control and monitor software development projects in the cloud environment.
  • You ensure that the projects are completed on time and cost as well as according to the requirements.
  • This also includes the definition of project objectives, the distribution of tasks, risk management and quality assurance.
  • You maintain close relationships with internal and external stakeholders, such as customers in the railway environment, partners or other departments within Siemens.
  • You understand the requirements of the stakeholders and ensure that the developed software solutions meet their needs.
  • You communicate project statuses and results to the stakeholders in an addressable manner.
  • You are the interface between development, engineering and product management as well as the customers.
  • You coordinate with these bodies and the core technical team and thus ensure a smooth project implementation.
  • You work closely with our international development team to develop innovative solutions in the railway environment.
