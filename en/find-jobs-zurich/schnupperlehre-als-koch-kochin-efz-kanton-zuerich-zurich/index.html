Role overview

Food is more than just being full – it stands for pleasure, creativity and real craftsmanship.

During your three-day trial apprenticeship, you will get an exciting insight into the chef/chef EFZ profession and experience how important taste, freshness and good timing are in the kitchen.

You take part in planning the day, checking out fresh ingredients, helping to prepare dishes and seeing how food is made – so that they not only taste good, but also inspire.
