Role overview

The Swiss Epilepsy Foundation (EPI) is a multi-sector company.

It includes the EPI WohnWerk, the Lengg High School, the donation Dapples, a seminar center with restaurant and the EPI Central Services.

In addition, the Swiss Epilepsy Foundation together with another foundation forms the sponsorship of Klinik Lengg AG, each with a clinic for epileptology and neurorehabilitation.
