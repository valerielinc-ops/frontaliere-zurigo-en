Mansioni In questa funzione, you are actively involved in the development of senior physician at the Wetzikon la ambulatory-policy care in the area of dependency diseases.

They conduct clarification interviews and accompany patients with dependency diseases and psychiatric comorbidities in their psychiatric-psychotherapeutic treatment, including opioid agonist therapies (OAT) as well as heroin-assisted treatment (HeGeBe), a technically demanding and socially significant field of activity.

They participate in the emergency, consular and liaison services of the supply region.
