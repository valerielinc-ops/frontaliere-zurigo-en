More about this search for cross-border applicants

Glarus, a picturesque canton in northeastern Switzerland, offers a unique blend of traditional Swiss charm and modern job opportunities. The job market in Glarus is diverse, with opportunities in sectors such as manufacturing, healthcare, and tourism. For cross-border workers, Glarus presents an attractive option due to its proximity to northern Italy, making it a feasible commute for those living in the Italian border regions.
