Role overview

Canton of Zurich Financial Directorate Application Operation Manager/-in ICT 90% Welcome to the fascinating world of taxes!

Together with the municipal tax offices, the cantonal tax office collects taxes for the Confederation, the canton and the municipalities.

We support individuals and businesses throughout the tax collection process.

Around 800 qualified employees are committed to this at the Zurich Altstetten site.

With us, you can expect a complex IT environment with central specialist applications and systems around the tax collection process.

You contribute your technical know-how, take responsibility and are part of a team that together ensures stable and secure IT operations.

Your tasks

  • Operation and LifeCycle Management of specialist applications, IT systems, tools and services in the Cantonal Tax Office
  • Analysis of faults and fault states and initiation, implementation and monitoring of appropriate measures
  • Ensuring the availability, security and stability of applications, data and IT infrastructure
  • Technical support of service owners in 2nd-level IT operations as well as professional support of external employees
  • Further development of the IT infrastructure and preparation and maintenance of technical documentation
  • Active participation in various projects and interdisciplinary teams Your profile
  • Completed higher vocational training in computer science or equivalent qualification
  • Several years of experience in the operation of specialist applications, IT systems or complex IT infrastructures
  • In-depth practical knowledge of Unix/Linux systems and a good understanding of IT operations, availability and security
  • Additional certifications such as ITIL, HERMES, TOGAF, SAFe or Scrum are beneficial
  • Structured, independent and solution-oriented working method
  • Service-oriented personality with team spirit and good communication skills in German and technical English
  • Openness to change and interest in digitalization, agility and continuous development
  • Readiness to work outside office hours, for example during maintenance, installations or disruptions Our offer
  • Challenging and versatile tasks in an exciting and dynamic environment
  • Fair and reliable employer with attractive employment conditions
  • Flexible working hours, home office options, lunch checks and a modern workplace near Zurich-Altstetten train station Interested?

Then apply now – we are happy to meet you.
