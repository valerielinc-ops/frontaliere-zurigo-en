Original description

Work when others sleep? Then you are exactly right with us! With around 135 employees, RegioSpitex provides hospital-external care and support for the inhabitants of Dietikon, Schlieren and Urdorf. Since 2019, in cooperation with Spitex rechtss Limmattal, Spitex Uitikon and Spitex Birmensdorf Aesch, it has also been offering the Nachtspitex for the Dietikon district. To strengthen our team, we are looking for an immediately or by agreement Dipl. Nurse HF / FH for night service (hourly wage basis) Your multifaceted tasks are • Caring for people of all ages individually and in their usual environment • carry out medical technical tasks (e.g. infusion therapies, stoma care, vital monitoring, etc.) • Supporting and advising relatives • Maintaining electronic progress reports with various IT programs You bring with you • Completed training as a Dipl. Nurse HF/FH or equivalent with SRK recognition mandatory • Cat B driving licence mandatory (vehicle is provided) • Ability to perform the care competently under demanding conditions, to remain calm in rapidly changing situations and to initiate the right measures • Appreciative and open treatment of customers as well as strong social skills • Readiness to be alone in the company car in the evening and at night and to go to different households We offer you • Compatibility of private life and work • Working hours in pure night service (22.00 to 07.00) or combinable with late service (17.00 to 22.00) • A versatile and challenging workspace where you can use and develop your expertise and experience • A working environment characterized by respect and appreciation • A careful introduction to the various home care tasks • Attractive employment conditions and modern infrastructure • Generous training and education opportunities • All employee benefits Are you interested?
