Main responsibilities

  • Develop and execute Shopping strategy in collaboration with other product leaders across the company, identifying key growth opportunities and driving innovation.
  • Maintain a deep understanding of the competitive landscape and emerging trends in retail, including the latest technologies and consumer behavior.
