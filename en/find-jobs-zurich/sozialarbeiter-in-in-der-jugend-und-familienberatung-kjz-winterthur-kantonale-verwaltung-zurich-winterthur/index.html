Original description

Canton of Zurich Education Directorate Office for Youth and Vocational Guidance Social worker in youth and family counselling, kjz Winterthur, 60 - 80% The Office for Youth and Vocational Guidance provides, orders, manages and coordinates offers of child and youth assistance and career guidance.The kjz Winterthur (Children and Youth Assistance Centre) is a poly-valent consulting centre for families, parents, young people, children and young children and works on the basis of the Children and Youth Assistance Act of the Canton of Zurich. The youth and family counselling (JFB) of the kjz Winterthur is looking for a social worker in 60 - 80% by immediately or by agreement. As a supporter you take civil child protection measures, take child welfare measures on behalf of the KESB and advise families, children and young people along their orders. The consultations cover a wide range of topics concerning family organization, education, separation and divorce, visiting rights problems and child protection issues. There is a great deal of emphasis on good and close cooperation with authorities, schools, institutions and specialist bodies. • Requirements - Training at a Swiss University of Applied Sciences for Social Work - Experiences in youth and family assistance and legal social work - Optional additional training in systemic, solution-oriented counselling - empathy for people in special situations of life - negotiation-proof performance skills - resilient personality - good written knowledge • We offer - good introduction and coaching by the department head - collegial exchange of experts as well as intervisions and supervision - demanding, sun-sinking and independent Activity - Living work that takes place in the middle of the lives of a wide range of population groups - Supporting interdisciplinary team - Fair employment conditions according to cantonal guidelines Contact - Please contact the department head Emine Ülgen Kunz (Tel. 043 257 59 17) for further questions a
