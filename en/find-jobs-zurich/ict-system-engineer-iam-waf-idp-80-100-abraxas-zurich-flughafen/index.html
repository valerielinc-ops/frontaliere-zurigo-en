Role overview

Our customers in the BORS environment (police corps and blue light organizations) use and operate highly secure and state-of-the-art technologies.

Data security and high availability of applications play a central role - around the clock.

As an outsourcing partner, we are responsible for the further development and operation of critical IT infrastructures and their applications and support our customers with innovative solutions.

Our experienced specialist team is looking forward to your support!

As an ICT System Engineer, you manage the WAF/IDP infrastructure in the security-critical police environment.

You ensure stable, secure and efficient operation, support projects and contribute your know-how to the further development of our WAF/IDP systems.

Your role

  • Design, implementation and configuration of Web Application Firewall (WAF) and Identity Provider (IDP) solutions
  • Creating and fine-tuning Web Application Firewalls and the WAF Rule Engine
  • Integration of single sign-on (SSO), multi-factor authentication (MFA) and federated identities (SAML, OIDC) into the WAM infrastructure
  • Analysis of security logs, identification of attack patterns (false-positive reduction) and support in resolving security incidents
  • Ensuring compliance with service management and change management requirements
  • Responsibility for upgrades, patching and performance optimization of systems in a high availability environment
  • Advising application managers and developers on the secure connection of their services as well as close cooperation with the SOC (Security Operations Center), as well as external partners Your profile
  • Successfully completed training in computer science (HF, FH, Master) or a comparable qualification as well as several years of professional experience
  • Deep experience with market-leading WAF solutions
  • In-depth knowledge of access management systems
  • Expert knowledge in HTTP/S, SSL/TLS (certificate management), TCP/IP and modern authentication protocols (OAuth2, OpenID Connect, SAML 2.0)
  • Experience in performing vulnerability analysis and a deep understanding of web technologies (APIs, JSON, REST)
  • Independent, solution-oriented and structured working method
  • Teamwork, customer orientation, as well as commitment and willingness for continuous further education
  • Ability to act calmly and goal-oriented in critical situations and also be available outside regular working hours (e.g.
