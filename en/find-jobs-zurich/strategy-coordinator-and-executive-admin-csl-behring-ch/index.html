Role overview

The primary purpose of the Strategy & Corporate Development and Finance Coordinator is to provide comprehensive coordination, analyst and logistical support to the Strategy & Corporate Development.

This includes managing high profile external meeting coordination with investment banks, VCs, biotech and Pharma execs, internal meeting planning involving senior management and highly cross functional support, coordinating travel and event logistics, handling invoicing and expenses, and maintaining accurate records.

Additionally, the role includes providing administration and coordination support the Strategy & Corporate Development and Finance team and other designated business leaders in managing global activities.
