Role overview

You will become part of a growing actuarial consulting team that supports leading Swiss life and reinsurance companies in strategic transformation projects.

The team works at the interface of actuarial expertise, technology and business change, helping insurers modernize their core systems, processes and actuarial functions.

We combine deep technical knowledge with concrete project work and invest in thought leadership: our recent work on Markov-based inventory management systems was presented at the SAV station colloquium and is publicly available (pwc.ch/en/insights/risk/markov-models.html).
