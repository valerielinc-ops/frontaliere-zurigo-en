Role overview

For our location at Limmatplatz with approx. 2,500 employees are looking for support for the operational management of the technical and infrastructural facility management for the assigned buildings and facilities.

In your new role, you are jointly responsible for trouble-free and economical operation, immediate problem solving and user support in everyday life.
