In order to advance modern medicine and significantly improve the management of serious diseases, we have developed and successfully established a powerful new class of potent, specific and versatile small protein therapies, called DARPin therapeutics.

The continuous development of a broad portfolio of therapeutic product candidates in oncology, radio-ligand therapy and ophthalmology based on the company’s proprietary DARPin platform allowed a successful listing of the company on the SIX Swiss Exchange and Nasdaq (ticker: MOLN).

Company and context

  • Position description Position title Head of Process Development (80-100%) Job description To strengthen our CMC organization and support the advancement of our growing pipeline, we are seeking an experienced Head of Process Development (Director).
  • In this leadership role, you will be responsible for defining and driving end-to-end process development strategies across upstream (USP) and downstream (DSP) for DARPin therapeutics.
  • You will lead internal teams and collaborate closely with external CDMOs to ensure the development of robust, scalable and compliant manufacturing processes from early development through GMP production.
  • You will oversee technology transfers, GMP manufacturing activities, and contribute to regulatory submissions, while ensuring high-quality material supply for preclinical and clinical studies.
  • Requirements For this dynamic and highly strategic role, we are seeking a seasoned professional with the following qualifications and competencies:
  • PhD in Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, or a related field;
  • Extensive experience in biologics process development, covering downstream processing and ideally upstream processing;
  • Deep expertise in protein purification, process development, and scale-up;
  • Proven experience in technology transfer to CDMOs and oversight of GMP manufacturing campaigns;
  • Strong experience working in a GMP-regulated environment, including batch record review, deviations and change control;

Additional details

  • Proven leadership experience in building and leading high-performing teams within matrix (e.g. CMC team) and line management structures;
