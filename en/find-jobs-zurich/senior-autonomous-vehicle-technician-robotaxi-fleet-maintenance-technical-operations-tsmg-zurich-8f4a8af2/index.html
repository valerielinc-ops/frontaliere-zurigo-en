Role overview

TSMG is hiring Senior Autonomous Vehicle Technician (Robotaxi Fleet Maintenance & Technical Operations) for field operations in Zurich.

We are looking for experienced Senior Autonomous Vehicle Technicians to support the maintenance, diagnostics, repair, and operational readiness of autonomous vehicle fleets and robotaxi operations across Europe.

As a Senior Autonomous Vehicle Technician, you will be responsible for ensuring that electric and autonomous vehicles remain safe, reliable, and ready for daily deployment.

Main responsibilities

  • Below is the full Lever job description with responsibilities, requirements and practical project details. Key Responsibilities
  • Perform preventive and corrective maintenance on electric and autonomous vehicles used in robotaxi fleet operations.
  • Ensure vehicles are technically ready for daily deployment, testing, commercial operations, and autonomous ride-hailing services.
  • Conduct detailed vehicle inspections to support fleet readiness, vehicle uptime, and operational safety.
  • Diagnose and repair mechanical, electrical, electronic, and software-related vehicle issues.
  • Support maintenance workflows for high-availability autonomous vehicle fleets operating in shift-based or 24/7 environments.
  • Perform advanced diagnostics on EV platforms, ADAS systems, autonomous driving hardware, and vehicle electronic systems.
  • Use OEM diagnostic tools, service software, and technical procedures to identify root causes of vehicle faults.
  • Troubleshoot issues related to battery systems, drivetrains, braking systems, steering systems, sensors, cameras, wiring, and vehicle control modules.
  • Replace, repair, and validate vehicle components according to safety, quality, and technical standards.
