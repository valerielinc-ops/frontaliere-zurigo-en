Description

In Corporate Tax, we advise companies operating on a national and international basis in the light of relevant tax regulations and developments. This enables clients to set up efficient structures, consider the tax effects of possible business models, and manage tax risks. Working closely with colleagues across PwC's global network, the team combines technical tax expertise with a strong understanding of transactions, restructuring and business strategy to support clients in an increasingly complex and fast-moving environment.

  • Provide proactive tax advice to a diverse client portfolio, with a primary focus on private equity and real estate clients, on international tax implications related to mergers, acquisitions and restructurings.
  • Lead and coordinate multi-country M&A and restructuring projects, including tax due diligence, transaction and holding structuring, SPA review, tax modelling and post-completion tax advisory.
