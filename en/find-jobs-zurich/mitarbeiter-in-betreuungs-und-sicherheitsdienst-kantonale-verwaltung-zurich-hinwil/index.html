Role overview

Canton of Zurich Directorate of Justice and Home Affairs Justice and Reintegration Enforcement Institutions Zurich Staff Support and Security Service 80

100% Justice and Reintegration (JuWe) is responsible for the execution of sentences and measures as part of the Directorate of Justice and Home Affairs of the Canton of Zurich and for the execution of various types of detention.

For our 1,400 employees from around 40 professions, the successful social reintegration of people who have become guilty of criminal offences, the prevention of relapses, and the detention and preparation of the departure of persons without valid residence permits are of particular importance.

Main responsibilities
