Role overview

Are you driven to architect and scale high-performance product management practices across a complex, global organization? Do you have the entrepreneurial mindset to transform how corporate functions like HR, Supply Chain, Global Branding & Marketing, Communications and Real Estate deliver seamless experiences? Are you ready to navigate an evolving agile landscape to establish product strategy standards and pioneer AI-driven capabilities that drive fast, measurable impact? We are looking for a Product Manager to join our Group Human Resources & Corporate Services (GHRCS) team to drive the evolution of product management in the Capability Building Center of Excellence. Key Responsibilities:

  • Establish product management excellence with consistent standards across agile delivery streams by operationalising ‘what good looks like’ and enabling teams on the Product Delivery Framework
  • Empower product streams and crews in our product-led operating model by driving strategy development and strong OKR definition
  • Pioneer AI-driven product management practices, embedding artificial intelligence across the product management lifecycle from discovery to continuous improvement
  • Define role clarity, skills frameworks, and development pathways to strengthen product maturity
  • Shape the product community by facilitating targeted capability building with agile delivery teams
  • Partner closely with the Experience Design Center of Excellence to embed superior discovery and delivery practices into the daily work of cross-functional pods,
  • Independently deliver strategic initiatives in the Capability Building CoE
  • Navigate organizational complexity to remove structural impediments and accelerate agile product delivery across GHRCS
