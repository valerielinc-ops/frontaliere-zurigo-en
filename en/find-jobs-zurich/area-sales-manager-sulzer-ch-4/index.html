Role overview

Sulzer is a leading engineering company with a proud heritage of innovation.

Join our global team to grow your expertise and develop innovative solutions that enable a prosperous and more sustainable society.

The Mid-West Area Sales Manager (ASM) is the primary external representative of Sulzer’s Electro Mechanical Services (EMS) and is financially responsible for the Order Intake, Gross Profit Margin, and Operating Net Cash-Flow.
