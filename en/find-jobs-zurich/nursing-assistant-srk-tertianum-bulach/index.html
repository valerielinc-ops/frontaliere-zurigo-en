Original description

The beautiful residential and care centre Tertianum Glasi offers comfortable and safe living for senior citizens in Bülach. The offer includes 62 apartments for senior citizens and 40 single rooms in the nursing department. A public bistro with event space, which is open 7 days a week, completes the comprehensive offer at the Tertianum Glasi. It opened its doors in the summer of 2022. The senior apartments and nursing rooms are part of the new Glasi area, a bustling district with apartments, shops, offices, restaurants and a central meeting place. For our young house and our team, we are looking for one by September or by arrangement Care assistant/in 80-100% or for night service (50%) Their tasks • Support and guidance of the assigned residents • Independent implementation of basic care • Assumption of responsibilities as a caregiver following guidance • Accompaniment and support of residents in everyday life • Submission of oral and written report • Assistance with domestic activities • Maintenance documentation management services • Assignments in the in-house Spitex Your profile • Completed SRC nursing course with completed internship in long-term care • Hearty and humorous basic attitude • Joy of contact with older people • Secure communication in German (verbal and written) We enable a diverse and exciting area of responsibility in which you can contribute to the development of the company and shape a lot. We look forward to active and charismatic co-creators. Do you bring practical experience and are you imaginative? So we look forward to meeting you personally! For any questions, Damien Recht, Head of Care and Assistance, is at your disposal at the telephone number 044 572 30 30.
