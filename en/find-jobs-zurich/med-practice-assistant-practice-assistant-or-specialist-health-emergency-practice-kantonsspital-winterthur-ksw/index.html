Role overview

The Emergency Practice is part of the interdisciplinary Emergency Centre, which provides emergency care for a catchment area of 200,000 inhabitants.

Of the approximately 68,000 patients every year, around 17,000 are treated with minor illnesses and injuries in emergency practice.
