Job Description

To strengthen our growing private markets franchise, we are looking for a Swiss based (Senior) Private Markets Product Specialist.

We are searching for a candidate who shares our enthusiasm for delivering a first-class client experience through an engaging and proactive relationship approach. This is an excellent opportunity to join a growing, seasoned and entrepreneurial global team with strong personal and professional development opportunities.

Tasks and Responsibilities

  • Accountable for driving a strategic and hands-on distribution effort across our private markets solutions, focusing on EFG’s in-house private markets program and product suite of perpetual/evergreen funds
  • Strategic partner to Client Relationship Officers (CROs), Investment Counsellors (ICs) and their clients to increase overall allocations to the asset class
  • Responsible for impactful and tailor-made marketing content for in-person and written presentations, both internally and for our global client base
  • Foster the ongoing development of our private markets franchise by identifying client demands and trends to shape our future product offering
  • Design educational initiatives to drive asset class-specific learning excellence and impact across various stakeholder groups within EFG

Requirements and Qualifications

  • 5+ years working experience in distribution and/or sales within the private markets (including real estate) industry, preferably focused on private wealth clients
  • For senior-level role: 10+ years of relevant working experience required
  • Ambitious and self-motivation personality with the ability to work in a target driven high-performance culture while maintaining “down-to-earth” attitude
  • Entrepreneurial mindset with an enthusiastic, flexible and hands-on approach
  • Excellent communication, presentation and interpersonal skills, with proficiency in English and Italien (German beneficial)
  • Master’s degree in Finance or similar and further professional qualifications (CFA, CAIA, etc.)
  • Advanced proficiency in PowerPoint and Excel, with the ability to create impactful client-facing marketing material

Our Values

  • Accountability: Taking ownership for tasks and challenges, as well as seeking continuous improvement
  • Hands-on: Being proactive to rapidly deliver high-quality results
  • Passionate: Being committed and striving for excellence
  • Solution-driven: Focusing on client outcomes and treating clients fairly with a risk-aware mindset
  • Partnership-oriented: Promoting collaboration and teamwork. Working together with an entrepreneurial spirit.
