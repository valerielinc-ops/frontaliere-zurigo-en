Main responsibilities

  • Work with our Google Cloud dedicated and data boundary partner operators to design, build and roll out an integrated partner program to attract and retain a partner ecosystem.
  • Define and drive the execution of the Go-to-Market (GTM) strategy, including business development activities to drive business growth.
  • Identify, engage,recruit and develop key partners with deep relationships in target commercial sectors to bring qualified net new opportunities and drive consumption.
  • Drive the independent software vendor (ISV) partnership lifecycle, ensuring an ecosystem of ISVs is activated to drive net new business growth and address customer workload needs.
  • Identify and engage strategic systems integrators and advisory partners (e.g., GSIs) to guide regulated industry customers through complex data classification strategies and business case development for Google Dedicated Cloud.

Key requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience.
  • 10 years of experience in a Partner Development Manager or strategic
  • Experience building partner ecosystems with system integrators, advisors, ISVs, resellers.
