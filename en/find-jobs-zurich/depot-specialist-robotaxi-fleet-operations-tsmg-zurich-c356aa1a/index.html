Role overview

TSMG is hiring Depot Specialist (Robotaxi Fleet Operations) for field operations in Zurich.

We are looking for Depot Specialists to support the daily operations of autonomous vehicle (AV) and robotaxi fleets across Europe.

As a Depot Specialist, you will play a key role in ensuring autonomous vehicles are clean, fully charged, inspected, and operationally ready for daily deployment.

Main responsibilities

  • Below is the full Lever job description with responsibilities, requirements and practical project details. Key Responsibilities
  • Prepare autonomous vehicles for daily robotaxi fleet operations.
  • Ensure every vehicle is cleaned, inspected, charged, and deployment-ready before the start of each shift.
  • Verify the cleanliness and condition of cameras, LiDAR, radar, mirrors, sensors, windows, and vehicle interiors to support optimal self-driving system performance.
  • Perform pre-deployment and post-shift inspections following established operational procedures.
  • Monitor battery levels and charging schedules for electric autonomous vehicles.
  • Support daily robotaxi depot operations by coordinating vehicle movement, parking, charging, and staging.
  • Assist with vehicle dispatch and return processes.
  • Organize parking areas to maximize operational efficiency.
  • Maintain depot cleanliness and ensure work areas comply with safety standards.
