Role overview

As a member of the Compliance team, you will play a key role in delivering the Compliance mandate to Zurich’s Group Functions, with a particular focus on Dealing in Securities well as Anti-Trust and Fair Competition.

In support of Zurich’s strategic priorities, you will enable business and functional stakeholders to pursue their objectives in a compliant manner by providing pragmatic advice, helping to embed a strong compliance culture and independently assessing, monitoring and challenging compliance risks.

The role holder is responsible for contributing to the development, implementation and continuous enhancement of the compliance framework across the relevant businesses and functions, with a primary focus on Dealing in Securities, including relevant SIX requirements, as well as Anti-Trust and Fair Competition.
