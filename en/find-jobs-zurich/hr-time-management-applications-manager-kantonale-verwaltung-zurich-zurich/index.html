Role overview

Canton of Zurich Financial Directorate Office for Computer Science Application Manager /-in HR-Zeitwirtschaft 100% The Office for Computer Science (AFI) is the computer science competence center of the Canton of Zurich.

It provides comprehensive services in the area of central computer infrastructure and canton-wide applications for directorates, authorities, the administration of justice and independent institutions.

In charge of implementing the new Information and Communication Technology (ICT) strategy, the Office for Informatics accompanies and supports the Canton of Zurich in the digital world.

Time management is much more than coming and going.

Together, we ensure that the processes around working hours, absences and expenses work reliably for thousands of employees.

At the same time, we are shaping the future of HR-IT in the Canton of Zurich with new technologies and systems.

If you like technical challenges and are happy to participate, we look forward to your application.

Your tasks

  • Operation, further development and optimization of cantonal time management solutions
  • Analysis and implementation of professional requirements in technical solutions
  • Collaboration and partial project management in exciting projects around SAP HCM, time management and HR systems
  • Advising the Staff Office and other cantonal offices on technical issues
  • Coordination of internal departments and external partners
  • Participation in the further development of the HR system landscape as part of the digital transformation
  • Take on this exciting challenge, grow with us and become part of our team in technical application responsibility for time management.

Your profile

  • Higher education in computer science, business informatics or equivalent qualification
  • Experience in application management or in supporting complex business applications

  • Understanding technical relationships, interfaces and system integration
  • Experience in projects and enjoyment of working with different stakeholders
