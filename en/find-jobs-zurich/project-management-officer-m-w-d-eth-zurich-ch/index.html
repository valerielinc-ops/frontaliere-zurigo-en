Role overview

Mansioni Architect:in / Project Manager:in planning (m/w/d) 80%-100%, Zurich, temporary Descrizione ETH Zurich is launching a pioneering research project with a direct connection to building practice with the "Living Lab HIL".The HIL building on the Hönggerberg campus is becoming a real laboratory.

The construction that needs to be rehabilitated is being rebuilt and expanded.

In the coming years, professors from the Departments of Architecture (D-ARCH) as well as the Construction, Environment and Geomatics (D-BAUG) will research and plan the renovation of the HIL building in the sense of circular construction.

The aim is to develop sustainable design and construction in the existing building and to apply it in an exemplary manner to a real planning and construction project.

For the planning team of the Living Lab HIL, we are looking for an experienced architect who plans, coordinates and implements our complex and demanding sub-projects in an autonomous manner – from the renovation of the existing building to the increase of the HIL building to the development of innovative manor spaces.

They accompany the projects across all phases and cover the entire spectrum from planning to implementation on site.
