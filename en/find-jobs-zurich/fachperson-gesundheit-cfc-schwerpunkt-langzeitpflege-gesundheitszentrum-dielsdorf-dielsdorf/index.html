Role overview

Dielsdorf Health Center is a modern geriatric competence center with around 500 employees from 35 nations.

Our offer ranges from counselling for older people and their relatives to general and specialized long-term care and care at home.

With more than 200 beds, the inpatient range of general long-term care at the Dielsdorf site is complemented by specialized treatment pathways for rehabilitative acute and transitional care, palliative care, psychiatric geriatrics and the focus on dementia.
